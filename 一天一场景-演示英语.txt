一天一场景-演示英语
新产品发布会——介绍发布的新产品

商务热词抢先记
launch n.启动，推出
slim adj.小巧的
feature n.特点
high definition 高清
can't wait to do sth. 迫不及待地做……

课文
Host:	Good evening, everybody. We are here for the new launch of J50 digital camera. Now let's welcome product expert John to give you details about this new model.
John:	Thank you. Now holding in my hand is J50. As you can see, our stuff is always ultra-modern and ultra-cool.
Host:	Yeah. And also very slim, very small, very easy for users to carry.
John:	The J50 digital camera is also packed full of high-tech smart features. It has 10.1 mega pixels, a 5x optical zoom. a 3.5" touch screen LCD, optical steady shot, face detection high sensitivity features, high definition movie at 30fps and at 1280X720 resolution. And there are also some new amazing features for users.
Host:	I can't wait to hear!
