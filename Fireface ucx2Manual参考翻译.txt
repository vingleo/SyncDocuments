Safety Instructions and Proper use

Read the manual carefully and completely before using the device. Pay attention to the following information on how to use and operate the Fireface UCX II safely. Improper use can lead to loss of warranty claims (see warranty statement on page 82).

Proper Use
The Fireface UCX II is a digital interface for professional audio applications for use with CE ap- proved class B computers.

To comply with the European CE standard, the Fireface UCX II must be used with CE approved Class B computers. All connecting cables must be shielded.

The computer and all cables connected to the Fireface UCX II must be properly grounded. Operation with non-certified computers and cables may cause inter- ference to other devices as well as the Fireface UCX II.

Do not open chassis
No user serviceable parts inside. Refer service to qualified service personnel.

To reduce the risk of fire or electric shock do not expose this device to rain or moisture. Prevent moisture and water from entering the device. Never leave a pot with liquid on top of the device. Do not use this product near water, i. e. swimming pool, bathtub or wet basement. Danger of condensation inside – don't turn on before the device has reached room temperature.

Installation
Surface may become hot during operation – ensure sufficient ventilation.
Avoid direct sun light and do not place it near other sources of heat, like radi- ators or stoves. When mounting in a rack, leave some space between this device and others for ventilation.

Unauthorized servicing/repair voids warranty. Only use accessories specified by the manufacturer.

5. First Usage – Quick Start
5.1 Connectors – Controls – Display

The front of the Fireface UCX II features two instrument and microphone inputs, a stereo headphone output, a rotary encoder with push functionality, four buttons, a graphical colour display and two status LEDs.

The Neutrik combo sockets of the two Mic/Line inputs provide XLR and 6.3 mm / 1/4" TRS connection. They have LEDs for phantom power (48V) indication.

Inputs 3/4, INST/LINE, accept both a balanced line signal as well as an unbalanced instrument signal via 1/4" TRS plug.

The analog outputs 7 and 8 feed the headphones output Phones. This low impedance output of highest quality is able to drive headphones at higher levels undistorted, no matter if low or high impedance headphones are used.

5. 首次使用 – 快速入门
5.1 连接器 – 控件 – 显示屏

Fireface UCX II 的正面有两个乐器和麦克风输入、一个立体声耳机输出、一个带按压功能的旋转编码器、四个按钮、一个图形彩色显示屏和两个状态 LED。

The four keys and the encoder, the high-resolution and clear col- our display, and a well thought- out menu structure enable the user to quickly change and configure the device’s settings completely without a computer. 5.2 Quick Start
After the driver installation (chapter 6 / 13) connect the TRS jacks or the XLR inputs with the analog signal source. The input sensitivity of the rear inputs can be changed in TotalMix (Input Channel Settings, +13 dBu / +19 dBu and Gain), assuring the highest signal to noise ratio will be achieved. Also try to achieve an optimum input level by adjusting the source itself. Raise the source’s output level until the peak level meters in TotalMix reach about –3 dB.
5.2 快速启动
安装驱动程序后（第 6/13 章），将 TRS 插孔或 XLR 输入与模拟信号源连接。后置输入的输入灵敏度可在 TotalMix 中更改（输入通道设置、+13 dBu / +19 dBu 和增益），确保实现最高的信噪比。还可以尝试通过调整源本身来实现最佳输入电平。提高源的输出电平，直到 TotalMix 中的峰值电平表达到约 -3 dB。

The signal level of the front inputs can also be optimized directly at the Fireface. The key MIC/GAIN gives direct access to this setting, which is then controlled by the encoder VALUE.

前置输入的信号电平也可以直接在 Fireface 上进行优化。MIC/GAIN 键可直接访问此设置，然后由编码器 VALUE 进行控制。

The digital outputs of the Fireface UCX II provide AES/EBU, SPDIF coaxial, SPDIF optical and ADAT optical at the respective ports.

Fireface UCX II 的数字输出在相应的端口提供 AES/EBU、SPDIF 同轴、SPDIF 光纤和 ADAT 光纤。

On the analog playback side (the DA side), a coarse adjustment of the analog output level at the rear jacks is again available in TotalMix (Out- put Channel Settings, Level), or the Channel Settings directly at the device. The big encoder VALUE controls the Main Out volume (de- fault: Line Out 1/2).

在模拟播放端（DA 端），后插孔的模拟输出电平的粗调同样可以在 TotalMix（输出通道设置、电平）或设备上的通道设置中进行。大编码器 VALUE 控制主输出音量（默认：线路输出 1/2）。

The output signal of channels 7/8, Phones, can also be controlled directly from the VALUE encoder.

通道 7/8、耳机的输出信号也可以直接从 VALUE 编码器控制。

The Fireface UCX II can store and load its current state in 6 different memory slots, called Setups (SETUP/REV, Options, Load/Store all Settings). With this, the Fireface UCX II can be used stand-alone after setting it up accordingly, replacing lots of dedicated devices (see chapter 24).

Fireface UCX II 可以在 6 个不同的内存插槽中存储和加载其当前状态，称为设置（SETUP/REV、选项、加载/存储所有设置）。这样，Fireface UCX II 可以在进行相应设置后独立使用，取代许多专用设备（参见第 24 章）。

In online mode some settings are greyed out, as these should be changed only at the computer, in the Settings dialog or in TotalMix FX. These include the choice of sample rate and the mix settings.

在线模式下，某些设置会变灰，因为这些设置只能在计算机上、在“设置”对话框或 TotalMix FX 中更改。这些设置包括采样率的选择和混音设置。

5.3 Menu and Navigation

The Fireface UCX II has a simple and clear menu structure, for fast and efficient operation directly at the device. In most cases, however, all settings will be done via the Settings dialog and TotalMix FX of the host computer. The operation on the device is typically limited to a direct adjustment of the monitoring volume (loudspeaker and headphones), the microphone gain, and DURec (Direct USB Recording). In stand-alone mode, all settings are available directly on the device.

6. Hardware, Driver and Firmware Installation
6.1 Hardware and Driver Installation

To simplify installation it is recommended to first install the drivers before the unit is connected to the computer. But it will also work the other way round.

为了简化安装，建议先安装驱动程序，然后再将设备连接到计算机。但反过来也可以。

RME is constantly improving the drivers. Please download the latest drivers from the RME website via http://rme.to/downloads. Unzip the downloaded file and start the driver installation with rmeinstaller.exe.

RME 不断改进驱动程序。请通过 RME 网站 http://rme.to/downloads 下载最新驱动程序。解压下载的文件并使用 rmeinstaller.exe 开始安装驱动程序。

Start rmeinstaller.exe and follow the instructions of the installer. After installation connect computer and UCX II using a USB 2 cable. Windows detects the new hardware as Fireface UCX II and installs the drivers automatically.

启动 rmeinstaller.exe 并按照安装程序的说明进行操作。安装后，使用 USB 2 电缆连接计算机和 UCX II。Windows 检测到新硬件为 Fireface UCX II 并自动安装驱动程序。

After a reboot, the icons of TotalMix FX and Settings dialog appear in the notification area. If not a click on the chevron leads to the settings that control the icon display.

重启后，TotalMix FX 和“设置”对话框的图标会出现在通知区域中。如果没有，单击 V 形图标将进入控制图标显示的设置。

Driver Updates do not require to remove the existing drivers. Simply install the new driver over the existing one.

驱动程序更新不需要删除现有的驱动程序。只需在现有驱动程序上安装新驱动程序即可。

6.2 De-Installing the Drivers

6.2 卸载驱动程序

8.3 Channel Count under WDM
The Fireface ADAT optical ports support sample rates of up to 192 kHz. For this to work single- channel data is spread to two or four ADAT channels using the Sample Multiplexing technique.
This reduces the number of available ADAT channels from 8 to 4 or 2 per ADAT port.

8.3 WDM 下的通道数
Fireface ADAT 光纤端口支持高达 192 kHz 的采样率。为此，使用采样复用技术将单通道数据扩展到两个或四个 ADAT 通道。
这会将每个 ADAT 端口可用的 ADAT 通道数从 8 个减少到 4 个或 2 个。

Whenever the Fireface changes into Double Speed (88.2/96 kHz) or Quad Speed mode (176.4/192 kHz) all devices no longer available vanish automatically.

每当 Fireface 变为双倍速度（88.2/96 kHz）或四倍速度模式（176.4/192 kHz）时，所有不再可用的设备都会自动消失。

8.4 Multi-client Operation
RME audio interfaces support multi-client operation. Several programs can be used at the same time. The formats ASIO and WDM can even be used on the same playback channels simultane- ously. But as WDM uses a real-time sample rate conversion (ASIO does not), all active ASIO software has to use the same sample rate.

8.4 多客户端操作
RME 音频接口支持多客户端操作。可同时使用多个程序。ASIO 和 WDM 格式甚至可以同时在相同的播放通道上使用。但由于 WDM 使用实时采样率转换（ASIO 不使用），因此所有活动的 ASIO 软件都必须使用相同的采样率。

However, a better overview is maintained by using the channels exclusively. This is no limitation at all, because TotalMix allows for any output routing, and therefore a playback of multiple soft- ware on the same hardware outputs.

但是，通过专门使用通道可以保持更好的概览。这完全没有限制，因为 TotalMix 允许任何输出路由，因此可以在相同的硬件输出上播放多个软件。

Inputs can be used from an unlimited number of WDM and ASIO software at the same time, as the driver simply sends the data to all applications simultaneously.

由于驱动程序只需将数据同时发送到所有应用程序，因此可以同时使用来自无限数量的 WDM 和 ASIO 软件的输入。

RME's sophisticated tool DIGICheck is an exception to this rule. It operates like an ASIO host,
using a special technique to access playback channels directly. Therefore DIGICheck is able to analyse and display playback data from any software, no matter which format it uses.

RME 的复杂工具 DIGICheck 是这一规则的一个例外。它的运行方式类似于 ASIO 主机，
使用特殊技术直接访问播放通道。因此，无论软件使用哪种格式，DIGICheck 都能够分析和显示来自任何软件的播放数据。

8.5 Analog Recording
For recordings via the analog inputs the corresponding record device has to be chosen (Fireface UCX II Analog (x+x)).

8.5 模拟录音
对于通过模拟输入进行录音，必须选择相应的录音设备（Fireface UCX II Analog (x+x)）。

The input sensitivity of the rear inputs can be changed in two steps and with adjustable gain in TotalMix (Input Channel Settings, Level), assuring the highest signal to noise ratio will be achieved. A further optimization can be achieved by adjusting the source itself. Raise the source’s output level until the peak level meters in TotalMix reach about –3 dB.

后置输入的输入灵敏度可以分两步更改，TotalMix 中的增益可调（输入通道设置、电平），确保实现最高的信噪比。通过调整源本身可以实现进一步的优化。提高源的输出电平，直到 TotalMix 中的峰值电平表达到约 -3 dB。

The level of the front-side analog inputs can be optimized via TotalMix (Input Channel Settings, Gain), or directly at the Fireface UCX II by the key Mic/Gain and the encoders 1/2. The multi- colour level meters shown on the display also provide useful information about the current level state.

可以通过 TotalMix（输入通道设置、增益）或直接在 Fireface UCX II 上通过按键 Mic/Gain 和编码器 1/2 优化前端模拟输入的电平。显示屏上显示的多色电平表还提供有关当前电平状态的有用信息。

Further information is found in chapter 19.
更多信息请参见第 19 章。

It often makes sense to monitor the input signal or send it directly to the output. This can be done at zero latency using TotalMix FX (see chapter 25).

监控输入信号或将其直接发送到输出通常很有意义。这可以使用 TotalMix FX 以零延迟完成（参见第 25 章）。

An automated control of real-time monitoring can be achieved by Steinberg’s ASIO protocol with RME’s ASIO drivers and all ASIO 2.0 compatible programs. When 'ASIO Direct Monitoring' has been switched on, the input signal is routed in real-time to the output whenever a recording is started (punch-in).

Steinberg 的 ASIO 协议与 RME 的 ASIO 驱动程序以及所有 ASIO 2.0 兼容程序可实现实时监控的自动控制。打开“ASIO 直接监控”后，只要开始录音（插入），输入信号就会实时路由到输出。

8.6 Digital Recording
Unlike analog soundcards which produce empty wave files (or noise) when no input signal is present, digital interfaces always need a valid input signal to start recording.

8.6 数字录音
与模拟声卡不同，模拟声卡在没有输入信号时会产生空的波形文件（或噪音），而数字接口始终需要有效的输入信号才能开始录音。

Taking this into account, RME added a compre- hensive I/O signal status display to the Fireface UCX II, showing sample frequency, lock and sync status for every input, and several statusfields in the unit’s display.

考虑到这一点，RME 为 Fireface UCX II 添加了全面的 I/O 信号状态显示，显示每个输入的采样频率、锁定和同步状态、以及设备显示屏中的多个状态字段。

The sample frequency shown in the fields Clock Mode and Input Status of the Settings dialog is useful as a quick display of the current configura- tion of the unit and the connected external equip- ment. If no sample frequency is recognized, it will read ‘No Lock’.

设置对话框中的时钟模式和输入状态字段中显示的采样频率有助于快速显示设备和所连接外部设备的当前配置。如果未识别采样频率，则会显示“无锁定”。

This way, configuring any suitable audio application for digital recording is simple. After selecting the correct input, Fireface UCX II displays the current sample frequency. This parameter can then be changed in the application’s audio attributes (or similar) dialog.

这样，配置任何适合数字录音的音频应用程序都很简单。选择正确的输入后，Fireface UCX II 将显示当前采样频率。然后可以在应用程序的音频属性（或类似）对话框中更改此参数。

8.7 Clock Modes - Synchronization
In the digital world, all devices must be either Master (clock source) or Slave (clock receiver).
Whenever several devices are linked within a system, there must always be a single master clock.

8.7 时钟模式 - 同步
在数字世界中，所有设备必须是主设备（时钟源）或从设备（时钟接收器）。
每当系统中连接多个设备时，必须始终有一个主时钟。

! A digital system can only have one master! If the Fireface’s clock mode is set to 'Master', all other devices must be set to ‘Slave’.

！数字系统只能有一个主设备！如果 Fireface 的时钟模式设置为“主设备”，则所有其他设备必须设置为“从设备”。

The Fireface UCX II utilizes a very user-friendly, intelligent clock control, called AutoSync. In AutoSync mode, the system constantly scans the digital input for a valid signal. If any valid signal is found, the Fireface switches from the internal quartz (Clock Source – Current Internal) to a clock extracted from the input signal (Clock Source – Current ADAT, SPDIF, AES or Word). The difference to a usual slave mode is that whenever the clock reference fails, the system will auto- matically use its internal clock and operate in clock mode Master.

Fireface UCX II 采用非常用户友好的智能时钟控制，称为 AutoSync。在 AutoSync 模式下，系统不断扫描数字输入以查找有效信号。如果发现任何有效信号，Fireface 将从内部石英（时钟源 - 当前内部）切换到从输入信号中提取的时钟（时钟源 - 当前 ADAT、SPDIF、AES 或 Word）。与通常的从属模式的区别在于，每当时钟参考失败时，系统将自动使用其内部时钟并在主时钟模式下运行。

AutoSync guarantees that record and record-while-play will always work correctly. In certain cases however, AutoSync may cause feedback in the digital carrier, so synchronization breaks down. To remedy this, switch the Fireface clock mode to ‘Internal’.

AutoSync 可确保录制和边录边放功能始终正常工作。但在某些情况下，AutoSync 可能会导致数字载体出现反馈，从而导致同步中断。要解决此问题，请将 Fireface 时钟模式切换为“内部”。

RME’s exclusive SyncCheck technology enables an easy to use check and display of the current clock status. SyncCheck indicates whether there is a valid signal (Lock, No Lock) for each input (Word Clock, SPDIF, ADAT, AES), or if there is a valid and synchronous signal (Sync). See chap- ter 40.1.

RME 独有的 SyncCheck 技术可以轻松检查和显示当前时钟状态。SyncCheck 指示每个输入（字时钟、SPDIF、ADAT、AES）是否有有效信号（锁定、无锁定），或者是否有有效同步信号（同步）。请参阅第 40.1 章。

Via Clock Source a preferred input can be defined. As long as the Fireface sees a valid signal there, this input will be designated as the sync source, otherwise the other inputs will be scanned in turn. If none of the inputs are receiving a valid signal, the Fireface automatically switches clock mode to ‘Internal’.

通过时钟源可以定义首选输入。只要 Fireface 看到有效信号，此输入将被指定为同步源，否则将依次扫描其他输入。如果没有输入接收到有效信号，Fireface 会自动将时钟模式切换为“内部”。

Under WDM the Fireface will (has to) set the sample rate. Therefore the error shown to the right can occur. An AES, SPDIF or ADAT signal with a sample rate of 48 kHz is used as sync source, but Windows audio had been set to 44100 Hz before. The red colored text label signals the error condition, and prompts the user to set 48000 Hz manually as sample rate.

在 WDM 下，Fireface 将（必须）设置采样率。因此可能会出现右侧所示的错误。采样率为 48 kHz 的 AES、SPDIF 或 ADAT 信号用作同步源，但 Windows 音频之前已设置为 44100 Hz。红色文本标签表示错误情况，并提示用户手动设置 48000 Hz 作为采样率。

Under ASIO the audio software sets the sample rate, so that such an error will usually not happen – but it can too. In slave mode the external sample rate has priority. Feeding 44.1 kHz will prevent the ASIO software to set 48 kHz – obviously, as the only way to do so would be to enter a different clock mode (Master/Internal).

在 ASIO 下，音频软件会设置采样率，因此通常不会发生此类错误 - 但也可能出现。在从属模式下，外部采样率具有优先权。输入 44.1 kHz 将阻止 ASIO 软件设置 48 kHz - 显然，因为这样做的唯一方法是进入不同的时钟模式（主/内部）。

In practice, SyncCheck provides the user with an easy way of checking whether all digital devices connected to the system are properly configured. With SyncCheck, finally anyone can master this common source of error, previously one of the most complex issues in the digital studio world.

实际上，SyncCheck 为用户提供了一种简单的方法来检查连接到系统的所有数字设备是否配置正确。有了 SyncCheck，终于任何人都可以掌握这个常见的错误来源，而这曾经是数字工作室领域最复杂的问题之一。

9.3 已知问题
如果计算机没有提供足够的 CPU 功率，和/或足够的 USB 或 PCI 总线传输速率，和/或足够的 PCIe 总线传输速率，则会出现中断、噼啪声和噪音。
在大多数情况下，在 Fireface UCX II 的“设置”对话框中增加缓冲区大小会有所帮助。还建议停用所有插件，以验证这些不是造成此类影响的原因。
更多信息请参见第 40.3 章。

Another common source of trouble is incorrect synchronization. ASIO does not support asynchro-nous operation, which means that the input and output signals not only have to use the same sample frequency, but also have to be in sync. All devices connected to the Fireface UCX II must be properly configured for full duplex operation. As long as SyncCheck (in the Settings dialog) only displays Lock instead of Sync, the devices have not been set up properly.

另一个常见的麻烦来源是同步不正确。 ASIO 不支持异步操作，这意味着输入和输出信号不仅必须使用相同的采样频率，还必须同步。 必须正确配置连接到 Fireface UCX II 的所有设备才能进行全双工操作。 只要 SyncCheck（在“设置”对话框中）仅显示“锁定”而不是“同步”，就表示设备设置不正确。

The same applies when using more than one Fireface UCX II - they all have to be in sync. Else a periodically repeated noise will be heard.

使用多个 Fireface UCX II 时也一样 - 它们必须同步。否则会听到周期性重复的噪音。

RME devices support ASIO Direct Monitoring (ADM). Please note that not all programs support ADM completely or error-free. The most often reported problem is the wrong behaviour of pano- rama in a stereo channel. Also try to avoid setting the TotalMix FX hardware outputs (third row) to mono mode. This will most likely break ADM compatibility.

RME 设备支持 ASIO 直接监控 (ADM)。请注意，并非所有程序都完全或无错误地支持 ADM。最常报告的问题是立体声通道中全景声的行为错误。还请尽量避免将 TotalMix FX 硬件输出（第三行）设置为单声道模式。这很可能会破坏 ADM 兼容性。

In case of a drift between audio and MIDI, or in case of a fixed deviation (MIDI notes placed close before or behind the correct position), the settings in Cubase/Nuendo have to be changed. At the time of print the option 'Use System Timestamp' should be activated. The Fireface UCX II sup- ports both MME MIDI and DirectMusic MIDI. It depends on the used application which one will work better.

如果音频和 MIDI 之间存在偏移，或者存在固定偏差（MIDI 音符放置在正确位置之前或之后），则必须更改 Cubase/Nuendo 中的设置。在打印时，应激活“使用系统时间戳”选项。Fireface UCX II 支持 MME MIDI 和 DirectMusic MIDI。这取决于使用的应用程序，哪一个会更好。

10. Using more than one Fireface UCX II
The driver supports up to three Fireface UCX II or a combination of up to three supported inter- faces. All units have to be in sync, i.e. have to receive valid sync information (either via word clock or by using AutoSync and feeding synchronized signals).

10. 使用多个 Fireface UCX II
驱动程序支持最多三个 Fireface UCX II 或最多三个受支持的接口的组合。所有设备必须同步，即必须接收有效的同步信息（通过字时钟或使用 AutoSync 并输入同步信号）。

● If one of the Firefaces is set to clock mode Master, all others have to be set to clock mode
AutoSync, and have to be synced from the master, for example by feeding word clock. The clock modes of all units have to be set up correctly in the Fireface Settings dialog.

● If all units are fed with a synchronous clock, i.e. all units show Sync in their Settings dialog, all channels can be used at once. This is especially easy to handle under ASIO, as the ASIO driver presents all units as one.

● 如果其中一个 Fireface 设置为时钟模式 Master，则所有其他 Fireface 都必须设置为时钟模式
AutoSync，并且必须与主设备同步，例如通过输入字时钟。必须在 Fireface 设置对话框中正确设置所有设备的时钟模式。

● 如果所有设备都使用同步时钟，即所有设备在其设置对话框中都显示 Sync，则可以同时使用所有通道。这在 ASIO 下尤其容易处理，因为 ASIO 驱动程序将所有设备作为一个整体呈现。


Please be aware that operating more than one Fireface UCX II may cause resource problems on
the computer side.

Note: TotalMix is part of the hardware of each Fireface. Up to three mixers are available, but these are separated and can't interchange data. Therefore a global mixer for all units is not possible.

请注意，操作多个 Fireface UCX II 可能会导致计算机端出现资源问题。

注意：TotalMix 是每个 Fireface 硬件的一部分。最多有三个混音器可用，但它们是分开的，无法交换数据。因此，无法为所有设备提供全局混音器。

20.3 DC-coupled Outputs (CV/Gate)
All eight analog outputs of the UCX II are DC-coupled. AC coupling via capacitor is common, both directly after the DAC chip and directly at the output socket. This prevents a fixed DC component at the output, which could cause negative effects such as popping noise or malfunctions in other devices that are also DC-coupled. However, it also serves as protection against dangerous DC from the outside.

20.3 直流耦合输出（CV/Gate）
UCX II 的所有八个模拟输出均为直流耦合。通过电容器的交流耦合很常见，直接在 DAC 芯片之后和直接在输出插座处进行。这可以防止输出端出现固定直流分量，这可能会导致负面影响，例如爆音或其他同样直流耦合的设备发生故障。但是，它也可以作为对来自外部的危险直流电的保护。

DC coupling has the obvious advantage of allowing the output to operate level-linearly down to 0 Hz. Even at extremely low bass (16 Hz) phase shifts are avoided. DC-coupled inputs are rare, especially for power amplifiers, so a possible DC offset of a playback normally does not cause serious issues.

直流耦合的明显优势在于允许输出以线性方式工作至 0 Hz。即使在极低的低音 (16 Hz) 下也可以避免相移。直流耦合输入很少见，尤其是对于功率放大器而言，因此播放中可能出现的直流偏移通常不会引起严重问题。

In order to realize the outputs stable and reliable (robust) as DC-coupled, the circuitry was de- signed for low DC-offsets and sufficient overvoltage protection. That this works has been proven for years, not only by RME's ADI series.

为了实现输出稳定可靠（坚固）的直流耦合，电路设计为低直流偏移和足够的过压保护。这种做法多年来已被证明有效，不仅仅是 RME 的 ADI 系列。

To be able to generate DC is quite useful in measurement technology, also in connection with audio. However, DC coupling is especially desirable in combination with analog, electronic sound generators (synthesizers). These have an input that can control the pitch as well as other func- tions via a DC voltage. Control Voltage, short CV, often also CV/Gate (gate determines tone on or off), CV Filter etc. is mostly based on the voltage range 0 to +5 Volt. If an audio interface can output such DC voltages then synthesizers can be controlled via suitable software and the analog outputs of the audio interface.

能够生成直流电在测量技术中非常有用，在音频方面也是如此。但是，直流耦合与模拟电子声音发生器（合成器）结合使用时尤其有用。这些合成器有一个输入端，可以通过直流电压控制音调和其他功能。控制电压、短 CV、通常还有 CV/Gate（门控决定音调的开启或关闭）、CV 滤波器等主要基于 0 到 +5 伏的电压范围。如果音频接口可以输出这样的直流电压，那么就可以通过合适的软件和音频接口的模拟输出来控制合成器。

The DA converters and analog output stages used in the UCX II have a very low DC drift and generate the DC voltages very reliably and reproducibly, so they are very well suited for this application.

UCX II 中使用的 DA 转换器和模拟输出级具有非常低的直流漂移，并且能够非常可靠且可重复地产生直流电压，因此非常适合此应用。

To receive signals in AES/EBU format, an adapter cable is required. Pins 2 and 3 of a fe- male XLR plug are connected individually to the two pins of a phono plug. The cable shield- ing is connected to pin 1 of the XLR and the ground contact of the phono plug.

要接收 AES/EBU 格式的信号，需要使用适配器电缆。母 XLR 插头的 2 和 3 针分别连接到唱机插头的两个针脚。电缆屏蔽层连接到 XLR 的 1 针和唱机插头的接地触点。

The optical input automatically switches to SPDIF operation when such a signal is detected. The audio information is then shown in TotalMix on the first two ADAT channels ADAT 1/2, which are channels 13 and 14.

当检测到此类信号时，光纤输入会自动切换到 SPDIF 操作。然后，音频信息显示在 TotalMix 的前两个 ADAT 通道 ADAT 1/2 上，即通道 13 和 14。

SPDIF coaxial Output
Apart from the audio data itself, digital signals in SPDIF or AES/EBU format have a header con- taining channel status information. False channel status is a common cause of malfunction. The Fireface UCX II ignores the received header and creates a totally new one for the output signal.

The output SPDIF coaxial can have the Channel Status Consumer or Professional, changed in the Settings dialog. When selecting Professional, the carrier level is also doubled for better AES/EBU compatibility.

输出 SPDIF 同轴电缆的通道状态可在“设置”对话框中更改，分别为“消费者”或“专业”。选择“专业”时，载波级别也会加倍，以实现更好的 AES/EBU 兼容性。

Operation as second AES/EBU Output
Professional AES/EBU equipment can be connected to the output SPDIF coaxial thanks to its format option Professional and a doubled output voltage. Output cables should have the same pinout as those used for input (see above), but with a male XLR plug instead of a female one.

作为第二个 AES/EBU 输出使用
专业 AES/EBU 设备可以连接到输出 SPDIF 同轴电缆，这得益于其专业格式选项和双倍输出电压。输出电缆的引脚排列应与输入电缆的引脚排列相同（见上文），但使用公 XLR 插头而不是母 XLR 插头。

SPDIF optical output
To send out SPDIF from the optical output, select the option Output Format Optical – SPDIF in the Settings dialog. The output signal in TotalMix has to be present on the ADAT channels 1/2.

SPDIF 光纤输出
要从光纤输出发送 SPDIF，请在“设置”对话框中选择“输出格式光纤 - SPDIF”选项。TotalMix 中的输出信号必须出现在 ADAT 通道 1/2 上。

！ Note that most consumer HiFi equipment (with optical or phono SPDIF inputs) will only accept signals in ‘Consumer’ format!

！请注意，大多数消费类 HiFi 设备（带有光纤或唱机 SPDIF 输入）只接受“消费者”格式的信号！

Therefore in SPDIF mode the optical output is fixed to Consumer status.

因此在 SPDIF 模式下光纤输出固定为消费者状态。

21.4 MIDI
Fireface UCX II offers one MIDI I/O via two 5-pin DIN sockets. The MIDI ports are added to the system by the driver. Using MIDI capable software, these ports can be accessed under the name UCX II MIDI Port.

21.4 MIDI
Fireface UCX II 通过两个 5 针 DIN 插座提供一个 MIDI I/O。驱动程序将 MIDI 端口添加到系统中。使用支持 MIDI 的软件，这些端口可通过名称 UCX II MIDI 端口进行访问。

The MIDI ports support multi-client operation. A MIDI input signal can be received from several programs at the same time. Even the MIDI output can be used by multiple programs simultane- ously. However, due to the limited bandwidth of MIDI, this kind of application will often show various problems.

MIDI 端口支持多客户端操作，一个 MIDI 输入信号可以同时被多个程序接收，甚至 MIDI 输出也可以同时被多个程序使用，但由于 MIDI 的带宽有限，这种应用经常会出现各种问题。

Note: The MIDI input indicator displays any kind of MIDI activity, including MIDI Clock, MTC and Active Sensing. The latter is sent by most keyboards every 0.3 seconds.

注意：MIDI 输入指示器显示任何类型的 MIDI 活动，包括 MIDI 时钟、MTC 和主动感应。大多数键盘每 0.3 秒发送一次主动感应。

22. Word Clock
22.1 Word Clock Input / Output
SteadyClock guarantees an excellent performance in all clock modes. Based on the highly effi- cient jitter suppression, the Fireface refreshes and cleans up any clock signal, and provides it as reference clock at the BNC output (see chapter 40.8).

22. 字时钟
22.1 字时钟输入/输出
SteadyClock 保证所有时钟模式下的出色性能。基于高效的抖动抑制，Fireface 可刷新和清理任何时钟信号，并将其作为 BNC 输出的参考时钟提供（参见第 40.8 章）。

The Fireface UCX II has one BNC socket with dual functionality – it can serve as word clock input OR word clock output. The current mode is set in the Settings dialog by the user.

Fireface UCX II 有一个 BNC 插座，具有双重功能 - 它可以用作字时钟输入或字时钟输出。当前模式由用户在“设置”对话框中设置。

Input Mode
The Fireface's word clock input is active when the BNC socket has has been switched to input mode in the Settings dialog, and a valid word clock signal is present. The signal at the BNC input can be Single, Double or Quad Speed, the Fireface UCX II automatically adapts to it. As soon as a valid signal is detected, the WCK field is lit, and the Settings dialog shows either Lock or Sync (see chapter 40.1).

输入模式
当 BNC 插座在“设置”对话框中切换为输入模式，并且存在有效的字时钟信号时，Fireface 的字时钟输入处于活动状态。BNC 输入端的信号可以是单速、双速或四速，Fireface UCX II 会自动适应。一旦检测到有效信号，WCK 字段就会亮起，并且“设置”对话框会显示“锁定”或“同步”（参见第 40.1 章）。

Thanks to RME's Signal Adaptation Circuit, the word clock input still works correctly even with heavily mis-shaped, dc-prone, too small or overshoot- prone signals. Thanks to automatic signal centering, 300 mV (0.3V) input level is sufficient in principle. An additional hysteresis reduces sensitivity to 1.0 V, so that over- and undershoots and high frequency disturbances don't cause a wrong trigger.

得益于 RME 的信号自适应电路，字时钟输入即使在严重失真、容易产生直流、信号过小或容易出现过冲的情况下仍能正常工作。得益于自动信号居中功能，300 mV (0.3V) 输入电平原则上就足够了。额外的滞后将灵敏度降低至 1.0 V，这样过冲和欠冲以及高频干扰就不会导致错误触发。

The Fireface UCX II word clock input can be high impedance or internally terminated (75 Ohms). The state is comfortably selected within the Settings dialog. To terminate the input check the option Word Clock In Termination under Options.

Fireface UCX II 字时钟输入可以是高阻抗或内部端接（75 欧姆）。可在“设置”对话框中轻松选择状态。要终止输入，请选中“选项”下的“字时钟输入端接”选项。

Output Mode
The same BNC socket can be used as word clock output instead of input, again configured in the Settings dialog. The word clock output is constantly active, providing the current sample frequency as word clock signal. As a result, in Master mode the provided word clock is defined by the cur- rently used software. In Slave mode the provided frequency is identical to the one present at the currently chosen clock source. When the current clock signal fails, the Fireface UCX II switches to Master mode and adjusts itself to the next, best matching frequency (44.1 kHz, 48 kHz etc.).

输出模式
相同的 BNC 插座可用作字时钟输出而不是输入，同样在“设置”对话框中进行配置。字时钟输出始终处于活动状态，提供当前采样频率作为字时钟信号。因此，在主模式下，提供的字时钟由当前使用的软件定义。在从属模式下，提供的频率与当前选择的时钟源的频率相同。当当前时钟信号失败时，Fireface UCX II 会切换到主模式并自行调整到下一个最佳匹配频率（44.1 kHz、48 kHz 等）。

Selecting Single Speed in the Settings dialog causes the output signal to always stay within the range of 32 kHz to 48 kHz. So at 96 kHz and 192 kHz sample rate, the output word clock is 48 kHz.

在“设置”对话框中选择“单速”会使输出信号始终保持在 32 kHz 至 48 kHz 的范围内。因此，在 96 kHz 和 192 kHz 采样率下，输出字时钟为 48 kHz。

Thanks to a low impedance, short circuit proof output, the Fireface delivers 4 Vpp to 75 Ohms. For wrong termination with 2 x 75 Ohms (37.5 Ohms), there are still 3.3 Vpp at the output.

由于具有低阻抗、防短路输出，Fireface 可将 4 Vpp 输出至 75 欧姆。如果错误地端接 2 x 75 欧姆（37.5 欧姆），输出端仍会有 3.3 Vpp。

22.2 Technical Description and Usage
In the analog domain one can connect any device to another device, a synchronization is not necessary. Digital audio is different. It uses a clock, the sample frequency. The signal can only be processed and transmitted when all participating devices share the same clock. If not, the signal will suffer from wrong samples, distortion, crackle sounds and drop outs.

22.2 技术说明和使用
在模拟域中，可以将任何设备连接到另一设备，无需同步。数字音频则不同。它使用时钟，即采样频率。只有当所有参与设备共享同一时钟时，才能处理和传输信号。否则，信号将遭受错误采样、失真、噼啪声和中断。

AES/EBU, SPDIF and ADAT are self-clocking, an additional word clock connection in principle isn't necessary. But when using more than one device simultaneously problems are likely to hap- pen. For example any self-clocking will not work in a loop cabling, when there is no 'master' (main clock) inside the loop. Additionally the clock of all participating devices has to be synchronous.

This is often impossible with devices limited to playback, for example CD players, as these have no SPDIF input, thus can't use the self clocking technique as clock reference.

AES/EBU、SPDIF 和 ADAT 是自时钟的，原则上不需要额外的字时钟连接。但是，当同时使用多个设备时，可能会出现问题。例如，当环路内没有“主时钟”（主时钟）时，任何自时钟都无法在环路布线中工作。此外，所有参与设备的时钟必须同步。

对于仅限于播放的设备（例如 CD 播放器），这通常是不可能的，因为它们没有 SPDIF 输入，因此不能使用自时钟技术作为时钟参考。

In a digital studio synchronisation is maintained by connecting all devices to a central sync source.

For example the mixing desk works as master and sends a reference signal, the word clock, to all other devices. Of course this will only work as long as all other devices are equipped with a word clock or sync input, thus being able to work as slave (some professional CD players indeed have a word clock input). Then all devices get the same clock and will work in every possible combination with each other.

在数字工作室中，同步是通过将所有设备连接到中央同步源来维持的。

例如，混音台作为主设备工作，并向所有其他设备发送参考信号（字时钟）。当然，这只有在所有其他设备都配备字时钟或同步输入时才有效，从而能够作为从设备工作（一些专业 CD 播放器确实有字时钟输入）。然后所有设备都会获得相同的时钟，并可以在所有可能的组合中工作。

！ A digital system can only have one master! If the Fireface's clock mode is set to 'Master', all other devices must be set to ‘Slave’.

！一个数字系统只能有一个主设备！如果 Fireface 的时钟模式设置为“主设备”，则所有其他设备必须设置为“从设备”。

But word clock is not only the 'great problem solver', it also has some disadvantages. The word clock is based on a fraction of the really needed clock. For example SPDIF: 44.1 kHz word clock (a simple square wave signal) has to be multiplied by 256 inside the device using a special PLL (to about 11.2 MHz). This signal then replaces the one from the quartz crystal. Big disadvantage: because of the high multiplication factor the reconstructed clock will have great deviations called jitter. The jitter of a word clock is typically 15 times higher as when using a quartz based clock.

但是字时钟不仅是“伟大的问题解决者”，它也有一些缺点。字时钟基于真正所需时钟的一小部分。例如 SPDIF：44.1 kHz 字时钟（简单的方波信号）必须在设备内部使用特殊 PLL 乘以 256（约 11.2 MHz）。然后，该信号取代石英晶体的信号。最大的缺点：由于倍增因子高，重建的时钟将有很大的偏差，称为抖动。字时钟的抖动通常比使用石英时钟时高 15 倍。

The actual end of these problems is offered by the SteadyClock technology of the Fireface UCX II. Combining the advantages of modern and fastest digital technology with analog filter tech- niques, re-gaining a low jitter clock signal of 22 MHz from a slow word clock of 44.1 kHz is no problem anymore. Additionally, jitter on the input signal is highly rejected, so that even in real world usage the re-gained clock signal is of highest quality.

Fireface UCX II 的 SteadyClock 技术真正解决了这些问题。结合现代和最快的数字技术与模拟滤波技术的优势，从 44.1 kHz 的慢速字时钟重新获得 22 MHz 的低抖动时钟信号不再是问题。此外，输入信号的抖动被高度抑制，因此即使在实际使用中，重新获得的时钟信号也是最高质量的。

22.3 Cabling and Termination
Word clock signals are usually distributed in the form of a network, split with BNC T-adapters and terminated with resistors. We recommend using off-the-shelf BNC cables to connect all devices, as this type of cable is used for most computer networks. You will find all the necessary compo- nents (T-adapters, terminators, cables) in most electronics and/or computer stores. The latter usually carries 50 Ohms components. The 75 Ohms components used for word clock are part of video technology (RG59).

22.3 布线和终端
字时钟信号通常以网络形式分布，用 BNC T 型适配器分离并用电阻器端接。我们建议使用现成的 BNC 电缆连接所有设备，因为这种类型的电缆用于大多数计算机网络。您可以在大多数电子和/或计算机商店中找到所有必要的组件（T 型适配器、终端器、电缆）。后者通常带有 50 欧姆组件。用于字时钟的 75 欧姆组件是视频技术 (RG59) 的一部分。

Ideally, the word clock signal is a 5 Volt square wave with the frequency of the sample rate, of which the harmonics go up to far above 500 kHz. To avoid voltage loss and reflections, both the cable itself and the terminating resistor at the end of the chain should have an impedance of 75 Ohm. If the voltage is too low, synchronization will fail. High frequency reflection effects can cause both jitter and sync failure.

理想情况下，字时钟信号是 5 伏方波，频率为采样率，其谐波远高于 500 kHz。为避免电压损失和反射，电缆本身和链末端的终端电阻都应具有 75 欧姆的阻抗。如果电压太低，同步将失败。高频反射效应可能导致抖动和同步失败。

Unfortunately there are still devices on the market which provide a word clock output that can only be called unsatisfactory. If the output breaks down to 3 Volts when terminating with 75 Ohms, you have to take into account that a device, of which the input only works from 2.8 Volts and above, does not function correctly already after 3 meter cable length. So it is not astonishing that because of the higher voltage, word clock networks are in some cases more stable and reliable if cables are not terminated at all. In some cases the 75 Ohms are built into the device and cannot be switched off. In this case the network load is often 2 x 75 Ohms, and the user is forced to buy a special word clock distributor (such a device is generally recommended for bigger studios).

不幸的是，市场上仍有一些设备提供的字时钟输出不能令人满意。如果在 75 欧姆端接时输出降至 3 伏，则必须考虑到，输入电压仅在 2.8 伏及以上时才有效的设备在 3 米电缆长度后就无法正常工作。因此，由于电压较高，如果电缆根本没有端接，字时钟网络在某些情况下会更稳定、更可靠，这并不奇怪。在某些情况下，75 欧姆内置于设备中，无法关闭。在这种情况下，网络负载通常为 2 x 75 欧姆，用户不得不购买特殊的字时钟分配器（这种设备通常建议用于较大的工作室）。

Therefore it seems of advantage to design outputs of word clock as low impedance types, but all word clock inputs as high impedance types, in order to not weaken the signal on the chain.

因此，将字时钟输出设计为低阻抗类型，但将所有字时钟输入设计为高阻抗类型似乎是有利的，以便不削弱链上的信号。

The Fireface's word clock input can be high-impedance or terminated internally, ensuring maxi- mum flexibility. If termination is necessary (e.g. because the Fireface is the last device in the chain), check the option Word Clock In Term. under Options (see chapter 7.1 / 14.1).

Fireface 的字时钟输入可以是高阻抗或内部端接，以确保最大的灵活性。如果需要端接（例如，因为 Fireface 是链中的最后一个设备），请选中选项下的“Word Clock In Term.”选项（参见第 7.1/14.1 章）。

In case the UCX II resides within a chain of devices receiving word clock, plug a T-adapter into its BNC input jack, and the cable supplying the word clock signal to one end of the adapter. Connect the free end to the next device in the chain via a further BNC cable. The last device in the chain should be terminated using another T-adapter and a 75 Ohm resistor (available as short BNC plug). Of course devices with internal termination do not need T-adaptor and terminator plug.

如果 UCX II 位于接收字时钟的设备链中，请将 T 型适配器插入其 BNC 输入插孔，并将提供字时钟信号的电缆插入适配器的一端。通过另一根 BNC 电缆将自由端连接到链中的下一个设备。链中的最后一个设备应使用另一个 T 型适配器和 75 欧姆电阻器（可用作短 BNC 插头）进行端接。当然，具有内部端接的设备不需要 T 型适配器和端接器插头。

22.4 Operation
The field WCK on the display will light up green as soon as a word clock signal is detected. To change to word clock as clock source, activate the Clock Source Word in the field Clock Mode within the Settings dialog. The status display Current changes to Word as soon as a valid signal is present at the BNC jack. This message has the same meaning as the green State field, but appears on the monitor, i.e. the user can check immediately whether a valid word clock signal is present and is currently being used.

22.4 操作
一旦检测到字时钟信号，显示屏上的 WCK 字段将亮起绿色。要将字时钟更改为时钟源，请在“设置”对话框中的“时钟模式”字段中激活“时钟源字”。一旦 BNC 插孔出现有效信号，状态显示“当前”将更改为“字”。此消息与绿色“状态”字段含义相同，但显示在显示器上，即用户可以立即检查是否存在有效的字时钟信号并且当前正在使用。

23. Operation and Usage
23.1 General
Using the rotary encoder, the four buttons and the clear colour display the Fireface UCX II can be configured and set up completely at the device. Additionally internal memory allows for the per- manent storage of six different states of the unit. Therefore the Fireface UCX II is able to operate fully stand-alone, without any connected computer. 23. 操作和使用

使用旋转编码器、四个按钮和清晰的彩色显示屏，可以在设备上完全配置和设置 Fireface UCX II。此外，内部存储器可以永久存储设备的六种不同状态。因此，Fireface UCX II 能够完全独立运行，无需连接任何计算机。在独立操作中，只需单击一个按钮，它就可以转换为完全不同的设备。此外，TotalMix FX 以及下面显示的应用示例也可以通过 MIDI 进行控制，请参阅第 28.6 章“独立 MIDI 控制”。

23.2 Settings at the Unit
The Fireface UCX II has a simple menu structure, depending on the button pressed.

MIC/GAIN. First push brings up the Mic.Gain 1/2, second push Line/Instr. Gain 3/4, third push returns to the meter screen.

REC/PLAY. Brings up the Record/Playback (Direct USB Recording) menu, second Rec/Play Set- tings, third push returns to the meter screen.

CHAN/MIX. First push brings up the Channel menu, second push the Mix to menu, third push returns to the meter screen.

The Mix to menu gives access to the whole submix functionality, mix destination and source. A source can be modified in mix level and panorama.

SETUP/REV. First push brings up the Options menu with access to the subpages Clock, Load/Store all Settings, Hardware/Diagnosis and Control Room. Second push brings up Re- verb/Echo with the subpages Reverb and Echo.

23.2 设备上的设置
Fireface UCX II 具有简单的菜单结构，具体取决于所按下的按钮。

MIC/GAIN。第一次按下可显示 Mic.Gain 1/2，第二次按下可显示 Line/Instr. Gain 3/4，第三次按下可返回到仪表屏幕。

REC/PLAY。显示 Record/Playback（直接 USB 录音）菜单，第二次按下 Rec/Play Settings，第三次按下可返回到仪表屏幕。

CHAN/MIX。第一次按下可显示 Channel 菜单，第二次按下可显示 Mix to 菜单，第三次按下可返回到仪表屏幕。

Mix to 菜单可访问整个子混音功能、混音目标和源。可以在混音级别和全景中修改源。

SETUP/REV。第一次按下可显示 Options 菜单，可访问子页面 Clock、Load/Store all Settings、Hardware/Diagnosis 和 Control Room。第二次按下可显示 Reverb/Echo，其中包含子页面 Reverb 和 Echo。

23.3 Store / Load Setups at the Unit

tings. First select the preferred memory slot, then the desired process (load or store). The

23.3 在设备上存储/加载设置
按下 SETUP/REV 键后，编码器将允许访问加载/存储所有设置子页面。首先选择首选内存插槽，然后选择所需的过程（加载或存储）。只需按下编码器一秒钟以上即可完成加载/存储过程。

UCX II 有六个内存插槽可供免费使用。三个固定插槽可方便快速地重置和配置设备：

出厂。将设备设置为出厂默认状态。

清除混音。执行 TotalMix FX 的完全重置。

23.4 Storing Setups from the Computer

25. Routing and Monitoring
25.1 Overview
The Fireface UCX II includes a powerful digital real-time mixer, the Fireface UCX II mixer, based on RME’s unique, sample-rate independent TotalMix technology. It allows for practically unlim- ited mixing and routing operations, with all inputs and playback channels simultaneously, to any hardware outputs. TotalMix FX adds 3-band parametric Equalizer, Low Cut, Echo, Reverb, Com- pressor, Expander and Auto Level.				
				
25. 路由和监控
25.1 概述
Fireface UCX II 包含一个功能强大的数字实时混音器，即 Fireface UCX II 混音器，它基于 RME 独特的、独立于采样率的 TotalMix 技术。它允许几乎无限地进行混音和路由操作，所有输入和播放通道同时连接到任何硬件输出。TotalMix FX 添加了 3 频段参数均衡器、低切、回声、混响、压缩器、扩展器和自动电平。

Here are some typical applications for TotalMix:
● Setting up delay-free submixes (headphone mixes). The Fireface UCX II allows for up to 10 fully independent stereo submixes. On an analog mixing desk, this would equal 20 Aux sends.

● Unlimited routing of inputs and outputs (free utilisation, patchbay functionality).

● Distributing signals to several outputs simultaneously. TotalMix offers state-of-the-art splitter and distributor functions.

● Simultaneous playback of different programs via a single stereo output. The ASIO multi-client driver supports the usage of several programs at the same time. If this happens on different playback channels TotalMix provides the means to mix and monitor these on a single stereo output.

● Mixing of the input signal to the playback signal (complete ASIO Direct Monitoring). RME is not only the pioneer of ADM, but also offers the most complete implementation of the ADM functions.

● Integration of external devices. Use TotalMix to insert external effects devices, be it in the playback or in the record path. Depending on the current application, the functionality equals insert or effects send and effects return, for example as used during real-time monitoring when adding some reverb to the vocals.

以下是 TotalMix 的一些典型应用：
● 设置无延迟子混音（耳机混音）。Fireface UCX II 允许最多 10 个完全独立的立体声子混音。在模拟混音台上，这相当于 20 个辅助发送。

● 无限的输入和输出路由（免费使用，配线架功能）。

● 同时将信号分配到多个输出。TotalMix 提供最先进的分离器和分配器功能。

● 通过单个立体声输出同时播放不同的程序。ASIO 多客户端驱动程序支持同时使用多个程序。如果这种情况发生在不同的播放通道上，TotalMix 提供在单个立体声输出上混合和监控这些通道的方法。

● 将输入信号混合到播放信号（完整的 ASIO 直接监控）。RME 不仅是 ADM 的先驱，而且还提供最完整的 ADM 功能实现。

● 外部设备集成。使用 TotalMix 插入外部效果设备，无论是在播放中还是在录音路径中。根据当前应用程序，功能相当于插入或效果发送和效果返回，例如在实时监控期间为人声添加一些混响时使用。

Every single input channel, playback channel and hardware output features a Peak and RMS level meter, calculated in hardware. These level displays are very useful to determine the pres- ence and routing destinations of the audio signals.

每个输入通道、播放通道和硬件输出都配有峰值和 RMS 电平表，由硬件计算得出。这些电平显示对于确定音频信号的存在和路由目的地非常有用。

For a better understanding of the TotalMix mixer you should know the following: 

● As shown in the block diagram (next page), the record signal usually stays un-altered. TotalMix does not reside within the record path, and does not change the record level or the audio data to be recorded (exceptions: EQ+D for Record and Loopback mode).

● The hardware input signal can be passed on as often as desired, even with different levels.

This is a big difference to conventional mixing desks, where the channel fader always controls the level for all routing destinations simultaneously.

● The level meters of input and playback channels are connected pre-fader, to be able to visually monitor where a signal is currently present. The level meters of the hardware outputs are connected post-fader, thus displaying the actual output level.

为了更好地理解 TotalMix 混音器，您应该了解以下内容：

● 如框图（下一页）所示，录音信号通常保持不变。TotalMix 不驻留在录音路径中，也不会更改录音电平或要录制的音频数据（例外：录音和环回模式的 EQ+D）。

● 硬件输入信号可以按需要多次传递，即使电平不同也可以。

这与传统混音台有很大不同，传统混音台的通道推子始终同时控制所有路由目的地的电平。

● 输入和播放通道的电平表连接在推子前，以便能够直观地监控信号当前存在的位置。硬件输出的电平表连接在推子后，从而显示实际输出电平。

In some cases it is necessary to synchronize the gain changes of these routings. 在某些情况下，需要同步这些路由的增益变化。一个例子是后推子功能，其中歌手音量的变化应与发送到混响设备的信号的音量变化相同，以便混响水平保持与原始信号的关系。另一个例子是路由到不同子混音的吉他信号，这意味着硬件输出在独奏部分时声音太大，因此需要同时降低所有输出的音量。单击 Trim 按钮后，可以轻松完成此操作，并获得完美的概览。

As all channel’s routings change simultaneously when Trim is active, this mode basically causes the same behaviour as a trim pot within the input channel, affecting the signal already before the mixer. That’s how this function got its name.

由于在启用 Trim 时所有通道的路由都会同时更改，因此此模式基本上会导致与输入通道内的微调电位器相同的行为，影响混频器之前的信号。这就是此功能名称的由来。

In the View Options / Show the function Trim Gains can be globally switched on and off for all channels. The global Trim mode is recommended when using TotalMix FX as live mixing desk.

在“查看选项/显示”中，可以全局打开和关闭所有通道的“修剪增益”功能。使用 TotalMix FX 作为现场混音台时，建议使用全局修剪模式。

The Context Menu. With a right click on the input, playback and output channels their context menus provide advanced functionality (these menus are also available in the Matrix, but only directly on the channel labels). The entries are self-explanatory and automatically adjust to where the click is performed. On all of these the first entry opens the Channel Layout dialog. The input channels offer Clear, Copy input, paste the input mix and paste its FX. On a playback channel Copy, Paste and Clear the playback mix are available. On an output channel Copy and Mirror functionality for the current submix and copying of the FX settings is offered.

上下文菜单。右键单击输入、播放和输出通道，其上下文菜单可提供高级功能（这些菜单也可在 Matrix 中使用，但只能直接在通道标签上使用）。条目一目了然，并会自动调整到单击的位置。在所有这些条目中，第一个条目打开“通道布局”对话框。输入通道提供清除、复制输入、粘贴输入混音和粘贴其 FX。在播放通道上，可以使用复制、粘贴和清除播放混音。在输出通道上，提供当前子混音的复制和镜像功能以及 FX 设置的复制。

25.3.1 Settings
A click on the tool symbol opens the channel’s Settings panel with differing elements. For exam- ple the option Inst exists only in input channels 3-4, and the ADAT channels do not offer the phantom power option.

25.3.1 设置
单击工具符号可打开通道的设置面板，其中包含不同的元素。例如，Inst 选项仅存在于输入通道 3-4 中，而 ADAT 通道不提供幻象电源选项。

Stereo. Switches the channel to mono or stereo mode.

48V. Activates phantom power at the corresponding input. Serves as power supply for high quality condenser mics. This option should stay off with other sources to prevent failure by spikes.

Inst. Switches inputs 3 and 4 individually to Instrument (Hi-Z) mode.

Gain (1-2). Sets the gain for the four front inputs. The knob can be ad- justed by dragging the mouse or by the mouse wheel. This also works when the mouse has been moved over the two gain displays. Then the gain can be changed separately for left and right channel, even when the channel is in stereo mode.

Gain (3-8). Additionally to the reference level setting, Line inputs 3 to 8 have an adjustable gain option from 0 dB to +12 dB.

Level. Sets the reference level individually for the Line inputs. The avail- able settings are +13 dBu and +19 dBu.

AutoSet. Activates an automatic gain reduction preventing overloads caused by too high gain settings. See chapter 19.2 for details.

Width. Defines the stereo width. 1.00 equals full stereo, 0.00 mono, -1.00 swapped channels.

FX Send. Setting the level of the signal sent to the FX bus which feeds Echo and Reverb. The current setting is also visible with closed channel settings, because knob and small fader are always synchronized. To make this function as useful as possible FX Send is locked to the highest submix, thus imitating the Aux Post Fader function of a standard mixing desk. When moving the big fader both knob and small fader will also move. This way the reverb signal will always have the same relation to the dry signal.

The level of the signal sent to the effects can be controlled by the FX In level meters of the FX window. It becomes visible after a click on FX in the View Options.

MS Proc. Activates M/S processing within the stereo channel. Monaural information is sent to the left channel, stereo information to the right.

Phase L. Inverts the phase of the left channel by 180°.

Phase R. Inverts the phase of the right channel by 180°.

Note: the functions Width, MS Proc, Phase L and Phase R affect all routings of the respective channel.

立体声。将通道切换为单声道或立体声模式。

48V。在相应的输入端激活幻象电源。用作高质量电容式麦克风的电源。此选项应与其他电源一起关闭，以防止啸叫故障。

乐器。将输入 3 和 4 分别切换到乐器 (Hi-Z) 模式。

增益 (1-2)。设置四个前置输入的增益。可以通过拖动鼠标或鼠标滚轮调整旋钮。当鼠标移到两个增益显示上时，这也有效。然后，即使通道处于立体声模式，也可以分别更改左右声道的增益。

增益 (3-8)。除了参考电平设置外，线路输入 3 至 8 还有一个可调增益选项，范围从 0 dB 到 +12 dB。

电平。单独设置线路输入的参考电平。可用设置为 +13 dBu 和 +19 dBu。

自动设置。激活自动增益降低，​​防止因增益设置过高而导致的过载。详情请参阅第 19.2 章。

宽度。定义立体声宽度。1.00 等于全立体声，0.00 等于单声道，-1.00 等于交换通道。

FX 发送。设置发送到 FX 总线的信号电平，该总线为回声和混响提供信号。当前设置在关闭通道设置时也可见，因为旋钮和小推子始终同步。为了使此功能尽可能有用，FX 发送被锁定到最高子混音，从而模仿标准混音台的 Aux Post Fader 功能。移动大推子时，旋钮和小推子也会移动。这样，混响信号将始终与干信号具有相同的关系。

发送到效果的信号电平可以通过 FX 窗口的 F​​X 输入电平表控制。在“查看选项”中单击 FX 后，它变为可见。

MS Proc。在立体声通道内激活 M/S 处理。单声道信息发送到左声道，立体声信息发送到右声道。

相位 L。将左声道的相位反转 180°。

相位 R。将右声道的相位反转 180°。

注意：宽度、MS Proc、相位 L 和相位 R 功能会影响相应声道的所有路由。

Besides Stereo/Mono, Phase L und Phase R the settings of the Hardware Outputs have further options: Level. Sets the reference levels of the 6 analog Line outputs. The available settings are +4 dBu, +13 dBu and +19 dBu. The Phones (channels 7-8) have a Low (+4 dBu) and High (+19 dBu) setting to choose from.

FX Return. The effect signal (Echo and Reverb) is mixed to the respective hardware output by the duo knob/small fader.

Crossfeed. Adjustable to 5 levels. Can be activated in a maximum of 4 stereo channels. Crossfeed uses the Bauer Binaural method for stereo loudspeaker simulation for headphones. This method, adopted from the ADI-2 series, creates a similar listening impression with headphones as with a normal loudspeaker setup.

Room EQ. Opens a window with 9-band Parametric Equalizer, Delay and Volume Calibration. See chapter 25.3.3.

Talkback. Activates this channel as receiver and output of the Talkback signal. This way Talkback can be sent to any outputs, not only the Phones in the Control Room section. Another application could be to send a cer- tain signal to specific outputs by the push of a button.

No Trim. Sometimes channels need to have a fixed routing and level, which should not be changed in any case. An example is the stereo mixdown for recording of a live show. With No Trim active, the routing to this output channel is excluded from the Trim Gains function, therefore is not changed unintentionally.

Loopback. Sends the output data to the driver as record data. The corresponding submix can be recorded then. This channel’s hardware input sends its data only to TotalMix, no longer to the recording software.

除了立体声/单声道、相位 L 和相位 R，硬件输出设置还有更多选项：电平。设置 6 个模拟线路输出的参考电平。可用设置为 +4 dBu、+13 dBu 和 +19 dBu。耳机（通道 7-8）有低（+4 dBu）和高（+19 dBu）设置可供选择。

FX 返回。效果信号（回声和混响）通过双旋钮/小推子混合到相应的硬件输出。

交叉馈送。可调至 5 个级别。最多可在 4 个立体声通道中激活。交叉馈送使用 Bauer 双耳方法为耳机模拟立体声扬声器。这种方法采用 ADI-2 系列，使用耳机可产生与普通扬声器设置类似的聆听效果。

房间均衡器。打开一个窗口，其中包含 9 波段参数均衡器、延迟和音量校准。请参阅第 25.3.3 章。

对讲。激活此通道作为对讲信号的接收器和输出。这样，对讲可以发送到任何输出，而不仅仅是控制室部分的耳机。另一个应用是按下按钮将特定信号发送到特定输出。

无修剪。有时通道需要有固定的路由和电平，在任何情况下都不应更改。一个例子是用于现场表演录制的立体声混音。在无修剪激活的情况下，到此输出通道的路由被排除在修剪增益功能之外，因此不会无意中更改。

环回。将输出数据作为记录数据发送到驱动程序。然后可以录制相应的子混音。此通道的硬件输入仅将其数据发送到 TotalMix，而不再发送到录制软件。

Another difference to the input and playback channels is the Cue button instead of Solo. A click on Cue sends the respective Hardware Output’s audio to the Main Out, or any of the Phones outputs (option Assign / Cue to in the Control Room section). With this any hardware output can be controlled and listened to through the monitoring output very conveniently.

输入和播放通道的另一个不同之处是 Cue 按钮而不是 Solo。单击 Cue 按钮可将相应硬件输出的音频发送到 Main Out 或任何耳机输出（控制室部分中的分配/提示选项）。这样，任何硬件输出都可以通过监控输出非常方便地进行控制和收听。

25.3.2 Equalizer
A click on EQ opens the Equalizer panel. It is available in all input and output channels, and affects all routings of the respective channel (pre fader). The panel includes a low cut and a 3- band parametric equalizer which can be activated separately.

25.3.2 均衡器
单击 EQ 可打开均衡器面板。它适用于所有输入和输出通道，并影响相应通道（推子前）的所有路由。面板包括低切和 3 波段参数均衡器，可单独激活。

EQ. Activated by the EQ button.

Type. Band 1 and 3 can operate in Bell (peak), Shelving (shelf), High-Pass and Low-Pass mode.

The middle band is fixed to Bell mode.

Gain. All three bands can change their amplification between +20 dB to -20 dB.

Freq. The filter’s center frequency is adjustable between 20 Hz and 20 kHz. While in Bell mode the center frequency is adjusted, in shelving mode the cut-off point (-3 dB) will be changed.

Q. The Quality factor of the filter is adjustable from 0.4 (wide) to 9.9 (narrow).

Lo Cut. Activated by the button Lo Cut. The slope of the high-pass filter is adjustable between 6, 12, 18 and 24 dB per octave. The cut-off point (-3 dB) is adjustable between 20 Hz and 500 Hz.

EQ。通过 EQ 按钮激活。

类型。频段 1 和 3 可在 Bell（峰值）、Shelving（搁架）、高通和低通模式下运行。

中间频段固定为 Bell 模式。

增益。所有三个频段的放大倍数均可在 +20 dB 至 -20 dB 之间改变。

频率。滤波器的中心频率可在 20 Hz 至 20 kHz 之间调整。在 Bell 模式下，中心频率可调整；在搁架模式下，截止点 (-3 dB) 将更改。

Q。滤波器的品质因数可在 0.4（宽）至 9.9（窄）之间调整。

Lo Cut。通过按钮 Lo Cut 激活。高通滤波器的斜率可在每倍频程 6、12、18 和 24 dB 之间调整。截止点 (-3 dB) 可在 20 Hz 至 500 Hz 之间调整。

The frequency graphics give a precise overview of the filter results. Overlapping filters influence each other. This can be used to achieve more than 20 dB amplitude, or to generate difficult frequency response optimizations.

频率图精确概述了滤波结果。重叠滤波器相互影响。这可用于实现超过 20 dB 的振幅，或生成困难的频率响应优化。

Note: TotalMix has an internal headroom of 24 dB. Extreme boosts with overlapping filters can therefore cause an internal overload. In any case such an overload is displayed by the Over LED of the channel’s level meter.

注意：TotalMix 的内部余量为 24 dB。因此，重叠滤波器的极端提升可能会导致内部过载。在任何情况下，这种过载都会通过通道电平表的 Over LED 显示。

Preset. Settings of the EQ and the Low Cut can be stored, loaded, and copied between channels at any time. A click on Preset opens a menu with several entries:

预设。可以随时存储、加载和在通道之间复制 EQ 和低切的设置。单击“预设”会打开一个包含多个条目的菜单：

▶ Recall: Presets stored before by the user can be selected and loaded
▶ Save to: There are 16 storage places available (EQ Preset 1 to 16)
▶ Import: Loads a previously stored TM EQ file (.tmeq)
▶ Export: Stores the current state as TM EQ file (.tmeq)
▶ Factory: Includes 14 examples for the creative use of Low Cut and Equalizer
▶ Reset: Resets the Low Cut and EQ to have zero influence (Gain 0 dB)
▶ Rename: The EQ Presets 1 to 16 can be renamed. The changes will be shown in both lists Recall and Save to.

▶ 调用：可选择并加载用户之前存储的预设
▶ 保存至：有 16 个存储位置可用（EQ 预设 1 至 16）
▶ 导入：加载之前存储的 TM EQ 文件 (.tmeq)
▶ 导出：将当前状态存储为 TM EQ 文件 (.tmeq)
▶ 出厂：包括 14 个示例，用于创造性地使用低切和均衡器
▶ 重置：重置低切和 EQ 以使其不产生影响（增益 0 dB）
▶ 重命名：可以重命名 EQ 预设 1 至 16。更改将显示在调用和保存至列表中。

Notes on the EQ Presets

Copy between channels: The EQ Preset menu of all channels is identical. If an EQ setup is stored via Save to on one of the 16 storage places, it is then also available in any other channel via Recall.

Copy between Snapshots: The Presets are not saved in Snapshots, therefore do not change when a different Snapshot is loaded. Presets are saved in Workspaces and also loaded with these.

Copy between Workspaces: is done by the export and import function of the Preset menu.

Important and useful Presets should always be saved as TM EQ file, so that they can not get lost even on accidental overwrite.

Factory Presets load like all Presets the settings of both Low Cut and EQ simultaneously. The current state, On or Off, is neither saved nor activated. Therefore a preset can be loaded anytime, but will not become active before the user switches EQ and/or LC on – except they had been active already before the load process. For this reason some Factory Presets have special names. For example Telephone (LC+EQ) will sound much better when the LC has been activated additionally to the EQ, as the LC here is set to an unusual high frequency of 500 Hz.

EQ 预设说明

在通道之间复制：所有通道的 EQ 预设菜单相同。如果通过“保存到”将 EQ 设置存储在 16 个存储位置之一，则也可以通过“调用”将其存储在任何其他通道中。

在快照之间复制：预设未保存在快照中，因此在加载其他快照时不会更改。预设保存在工作区中，并随工作区一起加载。

在工作区之间复制：通过预设菜单的导出和导入功能完成。

重要且有用的预设应始终保存为 TM EQ 文件，这样即使意外覆盖也不会丢失。

与所有预设一样，出厂预设会同时加载低切和 EQ 的设置。当前状态（开或关）既不保存也不激活。因此，可以随时加载预设，但在用户打开 EQ 和/或 LC 之前不会变为活动状态 - 除非它们在加载过程之前已经处于活动状态。因此，一些出厂预设有特殊名称。例如，当除 EQ 外还激活 LC 时，耳机 (LC+EQ) 的声音会好得多，因为此处的 LC 设置为 500 Hz 的异常高频率。

25.3.3 Room EQ
Almost everything written in the previous chapter also applies to the Room EQ. It is based on the technology and design of the 3-band PEQ, but offers 9 bands and is specifically intended for room correction. It is therefore only available in the output channels. As it is not part of the mixing as being more like a 'fixed speaker correction', its settings are only saved in the Workspace. The gain of the filters can be adjusted in steps of 0.1 dB (3-band PEQ: 0.5 dB).

25.3.3 室内均衡器
上一章中几乎所有内容也适用于室内均衡器。它基于 3 频段 PEQ 的技术和设计，但提供 9 个频段，专门用于房间校正。因此，它仅在输出通道中可用。由于它不是混音的一部分，更像是“固定扬声器校正”，因此其设置仅保存在工作区中。滤波器的增益可以以 0.1 dB 的步长进行调整（3 频段 PEQ：0.5 dB）。

▶ In the Options - Reset Mix menu, there are additional options: Clear channel effects without Room EQ and Total Reset without Room EQ.

▶ The Room EQ is located directly behind the 3-band PEQ and can be recorded via Loopback.

▶ The Room EQ preset files have the extension .tmreq and are not compatible with those of the 3-band EQ (.tmeq).

▶ A maximum of 16 channels of Room EQ or 8 stereo channels of Room EQ can be used.

▶ The button Room EQ only switches this one on and off. Delay and Vol.Cal are off at 0 ms and 0 dB, otherwise they are always on. This is is also signaled by the Room EQ button lighting up orange within in the channel settings panel.

▶ The Room EQ can work on mono and stereo output channels and can also be set differently for each channel, see screenshot below.

▶ 在选项 - 重置混音菜单中，有其他选项：清除无房间均衡器的通道效果和完全重置无房间均衡器。

▶ 房间均衡器位于 3 波段 PEQ 的正后方，可通过 Loopback 录制。

▶ 房间均衡器预设文件的扩展名为 .tmreq，与 3 波段均衡器 (.tmeq) 的扩展名不兼容。

▶ 最多可使用 16 个房间均衡器通道或 8 个房间均衡器立体声通道。

▶ 房间均衡器按钮仅可打开和关闭此功能。延迟和音量校准在 0 毫秒和 0 分贝时关闭，否则它们始终打开。这也通过通道设置面板中房间均衡器按钮亮起橙色来表示。

▶ 房间均衡器可在单声道和立体声输出通道上工作，也可以为每个通道进行不同的设置，请参见下面的屏幕截图。

Delay offers a delay of 0 ms up to 42 ms, in steps of 0.01 ms, at all supported sample rates.

Vol.Cal changes the channel output level in the range +3 dB to -24 dB, in steps of 0.1 dB.

Quick Configuration Tips Gain to 0 dB: Ctrl - left mouse button. Any parameter values: Double-click and type in the value.

Quick adjustment via mouse or mouse wheel: Hold down Shift.

延迟在所有支持的采样率下提供 0 毫秒至 42 毫秒的延迟，步长为 0.01 毫秒。

Vol.Cal 可在 +3 dB 至 -24 dB 范围内更改通道输出电平，步长为 0.1 dB。

快速配置提示 增益至 0 dB：Ctrl - 鼠标左键。任何参数值：双击并输入值。

通过鼠标或鼠标滚轮快速调整：按住 Shift。

25.3.4 Dynamics
A click on D opens the Dynamics panel with Compressor, Expander and Auto Level. They are available in all input and output channels, and affects all routings of the respective channel.

25.3.4 动态
单击 D 打开动态面板，其中包含压缩器、扩展器和自动电平。它们适用于所有输入和输出通道，并影响相应通道的所有路由。

Dynamics. Activated by this button.

Thresh. Threshold where Compressor or Expander start to work. The Compressor is adjustable from -60 dB to 0 dB, the Expander is adjustable from -99 dB to -20 dB.

Ratio. Ratio of input to output signal. Defines the intensity of the signal processing. Adjustable from 1 to 10.

Gain. Gain stage to compensate a loss in level caused by the Compressor. Adjustable from -30 to +30 dB. With inactive Compressor (Threshold 0 dB, Ratio 1:1) this function can also be used as universal digital gain stage.

Attack. Defines the rise time for the control voltage of Com- pressor/Expander. Adjustable from 0 ms to 200 ms.

Release. Defines the release time for the control voltage of Compressor and Expander. Adjusta- ble from 100 ms to 999 ms.

动态。通过此按钮激活。

阈值。压缩器或扩展器开始工作的阈值。压缩器可在 -60 dB 至 0 dB 之间调节，扩展器可在 -99 dB 至 -20 dB 之间调节。

比率。输入与输出信号的比率。定义信号处理的强度。可在 1 至 10 之间调节。

增益。增益级用于补偿压缩器造成的电平损失。可在 -30 至 +30 dB 之间调节。在压缩器未激活（阈值 0 dB，比率 1:1）的情况下，此功能也可用作通用数字增益级。

启动。定义压缩器/扩展器控制电压的上升时间。可在 0 ms 至 200 ms 之间调节。

释放。定义压缩器和扩展器控制电压的释放时间。可在 100 ms 至 999 ms 之间调节。

The amplitude graphics give a precise overview of the level changes caused by the current set- tings of Compressor and Expander. The white dot shows the level of the input signal and with this whether the signal is within the processing range or not. The small light blue lines in the level meter show the currently active gain reduction. Settings for these Dynamic Meters are found in the Preferences dialog (F2).

振幅图形精确地概述了由压缩器和扩展器的当前设置引起的电平变化。白点显示输入信号的电平，并由此显示信号是否在处理范围内。电平计中的小浅蓝色线显示当前活动的增益衰减。这些动态计的设置位于“首选项”对话框 (F2) 中。

Auto Level. Activated by this button.

Max. Gain. Defines the maximum amplification of the input signal. Adjustable from 0 dB to 18 dB.

Headroom. To minimize short-time overloads and clipping of the signal peaks Auto Level can be set to operate at a fixed offset to 0 dBFS. Adjustable from 3 dB to 12 dB.

Rise Time. Defines the speed of a level increase after a level reduction. Adjustable from 0.1 s to 9.9 s.

Preset. Includes the same functions as described on the previous page for the Equalizer. The names of the Factory Presets make clear whether Com- pressor, Auto Level or both (C+A) have to be switched on.

自动电平。通过此按钮激活。

最大增益。定义输入信号的最大放大倍数。可在 0 dB 至 18 dB 之间调节。

请注意，由于可用的 Smooth 和 Room Scale 控件，所有这些混响类型都非常灵活。它们中的大多数可以用作短混响和长混响，并且当 Smooth 调到最大或最小时，声音会完全不同。

TotalMix FX 为所有混响类型提供出厂预设和默认值，以确保快速设置时间和快速结果。请花时间用不同的信号和不同的设置进行试验，以了解它们的声音和用途有多么不同。

General Settings
PreDelay. Delay of the reverb signal. Adjustable from 0 ms up to 999 ms.

Low Cut. High-pass filter before the reverb generation, removes low frequency signals which should not cause a reverb sound. Adjustable from 20 Hz up to 500 Hz.

High Cut. Low-pass filter after the reverb generation. A reduction of the treble often lets the reverb sound more natural. Adjustable from 5 kHz up to 20 kHz.

Smooth. Softens the reverb effect, affects stereo width, density and sound colour. Adjustable from 0 up to 100.

Width. Adjusts the stereo width of the reverb signal from 100 (stereo) to 0 (mono).

Volume. Adjusts the level of the reverb effects signal sent to the FX return bus.

Special Settings for Room Types Room Scale. Determines the size of the room, thus changing density and length of the reverb effect. Adjustable from 0.5 up to 3.0.

常规设置
预延迟。混响信号的延迟。可从 0 毫秒调整至 999 毫秒。

低切。混响生成之前的高通滤波器，可消除不应引起混响声音的低频信号。可从 20 Hz 调整至 500 Hz。

高切。混响生成后的低通滤波器。降低高音通常会使混响听起来更自然。可从 5 kHz 调整至 20 kHz。

平滑。柔化混响效果，影响立体声宽度、密度和声音色彩。可从 0 调整至 100。

宽度。将混响信号的立体声宽度从 100（立体声）调整为 0（单声道）。

音量。调整发送到 FX 返回总线的混响效果信号的电平。

房间类型的特殊设置 房间比例。确定房间的大小，从而改变混响效果的密度和长度。可调范围为 0.5 至 3.0。

Special Settings for Envelope and Gated
Attack. Length of time for the volume increase of the reverb signal. Also called rise time. Adjust- able from 5 ms up to 400 ms.

Hold. Length of time for the fixed volume part of the reverb signal. Adjustable from 5 ms up to 400 ms.

Release. Length of time for the volume decrease of the reverb signal. Adjustable from 5 ms up to 400 ms.

Special Settings for Space Reverb Time. Sets the duration of the reverb referenced to a volume drop of 20 dB. Adjustable from 0.1 s up to 4.9 s.

High Damp. Sets the treble damping over time for the reverb signal. In principle, this is a low- pass filter like High Cut. Because of the operation principle of this reverb it shows a slightly differ- ent behaviour. Adjustable from 5 kHz up to 20 kHz.

包络和门控的特殊设置
攻击。混响信号音量增加的时间长度。也称为上升时间。可调范围为 5 毫秒至 400 毫秒。

保持。混响信号音量固定部分的时间长度。可调范围为 5 毫秒至 400 毫秒。

释放。混响信号音量减小的时间长度。可调范围为 5 毫秒至 400 毫秒。

空间混响时间的特殊设置。设置混响持续时间，参考音量下降 20 dB。可调范围为 0.1 秒至 4.9 秒。

高阻尼。设置混响信号的高音随时间衰减。原则上，这是一个低通滤波器，如高切。由于此混响的工作原理，它表现出略有不同的行为。可在 5 kHz 至 20 kHz 之间调节。

Echo. Activated by the On button.

Type. Lists different echo algorithms for selection. Available are: ▶ Stereo Echo. Separated echo generators on left and right channel. As a result the echo follows the sound source within the stereo field.

▶ Stereo Cross. Echo generator on left and right channel with cross coupled feedback which is only working for the stereo parts of the input signal. In case the input signal is only left or right the Stereo Cross acts exactly like the Pong Echo.

▶ Pong Echo. Generates an echo that jumps between left and right channel, independent from the source signal’s stereo position.

回声。通过“开启”按钮激活。

类型。列出不同的回声算法以供选择。可用的算法有：▶ 立体声回声。左右声道上的独立回声发生器。因此，回声在立体声场内跟随声源。

▶ 立体声交叉。左右声道上的回声发生器具有交叉耦合反馈，仅适用于输入信号的立体声部分。如果输入信号仅为左声道或右声道，则立体声交叉的作用与 Pong 回声完全相同。

▶ Pong 回声。生成在左右声道之间跳跃的回声，与源信号的立体声位置无关。


Settings
Delay Time. Sets the delay time for the first echo.

BPM. A double click on the lower value field offers a delay adjustment as Beats Per Minute.

Feedback. Feedback to produce further echoes.

Width. Adjusts the stereo width of the echo signal from 100 (stereo) to 0 (mono).

Volume. Adjusts the level of the echo effects signal sent to the FX return bus.

High Cut (HC). Option for more natural sound or intentional damping of the delayed signal.

设置
延迟时间。设置第一个回声的延迟时间。

BPM。双击较低的值字段可调整延迟时间，即每分钟节拍数。

反馈。反馈以产生进一步的回声。

宽度。将回声信号的立体声宽度从 100（立体声）调整为 0（单声道）。

音量。调整发送到 FX 返回总线的回声效果信号的级别。

高切 (HC)。用于更自然的声音或有意衰减延迟信号的选项。

Preset. Settings of the Reverb and the Echo can be stored, loaded, and copied between channels at any time. A click on Preset opens a menu with several entries:

▶ Recall: Presets stored before by the user can be selected and loaded
▶ Save to: There are 16 storage places available (Reverb/Echo Preset 1 to 16)
▶ Import: Loads a previously stored TM FX Reverb file (.tmrv) or a TM FX Echo file (.tmeo)
▶ Export: Stores the current state as TM FX Reverb file (.tmrv) or as TM FX Echo file (.tmeo)
▶ Factory: Includes 10 examples for the configuration of the Reverb
▶ Reset: Resets the Reverb or Echo
▶ Rename: The Presets 1 to 16 can be renamed. The changes will be shown in both lists Recall and Save to.

预设。混响和回声的设置可以随时存储、加载并在通道之间复制。单击“预设”将打开一个菜单，其中包含多个条目：

▶ 调用：可以选择和加载用户之前存储的预设
▶ 保存到：有 16 个存储位置可用（混响/回声预设 1 至 16）
▶ 导入：加载之前存储的 TM FX 混响文件 (.tmrv) 或 TM FX 回声文件 (.tmeo)
▶ 导出：将当前状态存储为 TM FX 混响文件 (.tmrv) 或 TM FX 回声文件 (.tmeo)

26. The Matrix
26.1 Overview

The mixer window of TotalMix looks and operates similar to mixing desks, as it is based on a conventional stereo design. The matrix display presents a different method of assigning and rout- ing channels, based on a single channel or monaural design. The matrix view of the Fireface UCX II has the look and works like a conventional patchbay, adding functionality way beyond compa- rable hardware and software solutions. While most patchbays will allow you to connect inputs to outputs with just the original level (1:1, or 0 dB, as known from mechanical patchbays), TotalMix allows you to use a freely definable gain value per crosspoint.

Matrix and TotalMix are different ways of displaying the same processes. Because of this both views are always fully synchronized. Each change in one view is immediately reflected in the other view as well.

26.2 Elements of the Matrix View
The visual design of the Matrix is determined by the architecture of the Fireface UCX II system:
▶ Horizontal labels. All hardware outputs
▶ Vertical labels. All hardware inputs. Below are all playback channels.
▶ Green 0.0 dB field. Standard 1:1 routing
▶ Dark grey field with number. Shows the current gain value as dB
▶ Blue field. This routing is muted
▶ Red field. Phase 180° (inverted)
▶ Dark grey field. No routing.

To maintain overview when the window size has been reduced, the labels are floating. They won't leave the visible area when scrolling. A right mouse click on the labels brings up the context menu with the exact same options as in the mixer view: Copy / Mirror / Paste /Clear input channels and submixes.

26. 矩阵
26.1 概述

TotalMix 的混音器窗口外观和操作方式与混音台类似，因为它基于传统的立体声设计。矩阵显示基于单通道或单声道设计，提供了一种不同的分配和路由通道的方法。Fireface UCX II 的矩阵视图外观和工作方式与传统配线架类似，但功能远远超出了可比的硬件和软件解决方案。虽然大多数配线架允许您仅使用原始电平（1:1 或 0 dB，如机械配线架所示）将输入连接到输出，但 TotalMix 允许您使用每个交叉点的可自由定义的增益值。

矩阵和 TotalMix 是显示相同过程的不同方式。因此，两个视图始终完全同步。一个视图中的每个更改也会立即反映在另一个视图中。

26.2 矩阵视图的元素
矩阵的视觉设计由 Fireface UCX II 系统的架构决定：
▶ 水平标签。所有硬件输出
▶ 垂直标签。所有硬件输入。下面是所有播放通道。
▶ 绿色 0.0 dB 字段。标准 1:1 路由
▶ 带数字的深灰色字段。以 dB 为单位显示当前增益值
▶ 蓝色字段。此路由已静音
▶ 红色字段。相位 180°（反转）
▶ 深灰色字段。无路由。

为了在窗口大小缩小时保持概览，标签是浮动的。滚动时它们不会离开可见区域。右键单击标签会弹出上下文菜单，其中包含与混音器视图中完全相同的选项：复制/镜像/粘贴/清除输入通道和子混音。

26.3 Operation
Using the Matrix is a breeze. It is very easy to indentify the current crosspoint, because the outer labels light up in orange according to the mouse position.
▶ If input 1 is to be routed to output 1, use the mouse and click one time on crosspoint In 1 / AN1 with held down Ctrl key. Two green 0.0 dB field pop in, another click removes them.
▶ To change the gain (equals the use of a different fader position, see simultaneous display of the mixer view), drag the mouse up or down, starting from the gain field. The value within the field changes accordingly. The corresponding fader in the mixer view is moving simultane- ously, in case the currently modified routing is visible.
▶ On the right side is the Control Strip from the mixer window, adapted to the Matrix. The button for the temporary fader group is missing as well as all View options, as they don’t make sense here. Instead the button Mono Mode lets you decide whether all the actions performed in the Matrix are valid for two channels or just one.

26.3 操作
使用矩阵非常简单。识别当前交叉点非常容易，因为外部标签会根据鼠标位置亮起橙色。
▶ 如果要将输入 1 路由到输出 1，请使用鼠标并按住 Ctrl 键单击交叉点 In 1 / AN1。两个绿色 0.0 dB 字段弹出，再次单击可将其删除。
▶ 要更改增益（相当于使用不同的推子位置，请参见混频器视图的同时显示），请从增益字段开始向上或向下拖动鼠标。字段内的值会相应更改。如果当前修改的路由可见，混频器视图中的相应推子会同时移动。
▶ 右侧是混频器窗口的控制条，适用于矩阵。临时推子组的按钮以及所有视图选项都缺失，因为它们在这里没有意义。相反，单声道模式按钮可以让您决定在矩阵中执行的所有操作是否对两个通道有效还是仅对一个通道有效。

The Matrix not always replaces the mixer view, but it significantly enhances the routing capabilities and - more important - is a brilliant way to get a fast overview of all active routings. It shows you in a glance what's going on. And since the Matrix operates monaural, it is very easy to set up specific routings with specific gains.
Further notes on operation
▶ A double click on a crosspoint toggles between 0.0 dB and mute.
▶ Holding down Ctrl while clicking on one of the surrounding labels, or a double click on these activates/deactivates mute for all horizontal or vertical crosspoints.
▶ Holding down Shift while clicking on one of the surrounding labels activates/deactivates Phase for all horizontal or vertical crosspoints.

Matrix 并非总是取代混频器视图，但它显著增强了路由功能，更重要的是，它是一种快速概览所有活动路由的绝妙方式。它一目了然地向您展示正在发生的事情。由于 Matrix 以单声道运行，因此可以非常轻松地设置具有特定增益的特定路由。
有关操作的更多说明

▶ 按住 Ctrl 并单击其中一个周围标签，或双击这些标签可激活/停用所有水平或垂直交叉点的静音。

27. Tips and Tricks
27.1 ASIO Direct Monitoring (Windows)
Programs that support ADM (ASIO Direct Monitoring - Samplitude, Sequoia, Cubase, Nuendo etc.) send control commands to TotalMix. This is directly shown by TotalMix. When a fader is moved in the ASIO host the corresponding fader in TotalMix will move too. TotalMix reflects all ADM gain and pan changes in real-time.
But: the faders only move when the currently activated routing (the selected submix) corresponds to the routing in the ASIO host. The Matrix on the other hand will show any change, as it shows all possible routings in one view.

27.2 Copy a Submix
TotalMix allows you to copy complete submixes to other outputs. In case a complex submix is need with only a few changes on a different output, the whole submix can be copied to that output.
Right click with the mouse on the original submix output, means Hardware Output. In the context menu select Copy Submix. Then right click on the new submix output, choose Paste Submix in the context menu. Now fine tune the submix.

27.3 Doubling the Output Signal (Mirror)
If a mix should be sent out via two (or more) different hardware outputs simply mirror that mix to any number of other outputs. A right click on the original output brings up the option to Copy/Mirror <name>. Another right click on the new output, then selecting Mirror of Output <name> will paste the whole submix and then synchronize it automatically to any future changes. The outputs now always send out the same signals, but their main volume (fader) and the EQ and Dynamics set- tings stay fully independent.

27.4 Delete a Submix
The easiest and quickest way to delete complex routings is by selection of the according output channel in the mixer view by a right mouse click, and selection of the menu entry Clear Submix.
As TotalMix FX includes an unlimited undo the delete process can be undone without any problem.

27. 提示和技巧
27.1 ASIO 直接监控（Windows）
支持 ADM（ASIO 直接监控 - Samplitude、Sequoia、Cubase、Nuendo 等）的程序会向 TotalMix 发送控制命令。TotalMix 会直接显示这些命令。当在 ASIO 主机中移动推子时，TotalMix 中相应的推子也会移动。TotalMix 会实时反映所有 ADM 增益和平移变化。
但是：只有当前激活的路由（选定的子混音）与 ASIO 主机中的路由相对应时，推子才会移动。另一方面，Matrix 会显示任何变化，因为它在一个视图中显示所有可能的路由。
27.2 复制子混音
TotalMix 允许您将完整的子混音复制到其他输出。如果需要一个复杂的子混音，而其他输出上只需进行少量更改，则可以将整个子混音复制到该输出。
用鼠标右键单击原始子混音输出，即硬件输出。在上下文菜单中选择复制子混音。然后右键单击新的子混音输出，在上下文菜单中选择粘贴子混音。现在微调子混音。

27.3 加倍输出信号（镜像）
如果混音应通过两个（或更多）不同的硬件输出发送，只需将该混音镜像到任意数量的其他输出。右键单击原始输出，将显示复制/镜像 <name> 选项。再次右键单击新输出，然后选择镜像输出 <name> 将粘贴整个子混音，然后将其自动同步到任何未来的更改。输出现在总是发出相同的信号，但它们的主音量（推子）和 EQ 和动态设置保持完全独立。

27.4 删除子混音
删除复杂路由的最简单和最快捷的方法是右键单击混音器视图中的相应输出通道，然后选择菜单项清除子混音。
由于 TotalMix FX 包含无限撤消，因此可以毫无问题地撤消删除过程。

27.5 Copy and Paste everywhere
The above three tips use functions found in the right click context menu available on all channels of the mixer view. These menus are also available in the Matrix, but only directly on the channel labels. They are self-explanatory and automatically adjust to where the click is performed. The input channels offer Clear, Copy input, Paste the input mix and Paste its FX. On a playback channel Copy, Paste and Clear the playback mix are available. On an output channel Copy and Mirror functionality for the current submix and copying of the FX settings is offered.
These options are very advanced and mighty tools to quickly do the impossible. Still there is no need to fear breaking something, as a simple click (or several) on the Undo button will get you back to where you started!

27.6 Recording a Submix - Loopback
TotalMix includes an internal loopback function, from the Hardware Outputs to the recording soft- ware. Instead of the signal at the hardware input, the signal at the hardware output is sent to the record software. This way, submixes can be recorded without an external loopback cable. Also the playback from a software can be recorded by another software.
The function is activated by the Loopback button in the Settings panel of the Hardware Outputs.
In loopback mode, the signal at the hardware input of the corresponding channel is no longer sent to the recording software, but still passed through to TotalMix. Therefore TotalMix can be used to route this input signal to any hardware output. Using the subgroup recording, the input can still be recorded on a different channel.
As each of the 10 stereo hardware outputs can be routed to the record software, and none of these hardware inputs get lost, TotalMix offers an overall flexibility and performance not rivalled by any other solution.
The risk of feedbacks, a basic problem of loopback methods, is low, because the feedback can not happen within the mixer, only when the audio software is switched into monitoring mode.

The block diagram shows how the software's input signal is played back, and fed back from the Hardware Output to the software input.

The block diagram also shows why with activated Loopback all PEQs etc of the Hardware Output are now within the record path. With Loopback active PEQ and Dyn of the input is not in the record path, only in the monitoring path, even when the Option DSP – EQ+D for Record is activated.

Recording a Software's playback
In real world application, recording a software's output with another software will show the follow- ing problem: The record software tries to open the same playback channel as the playback soft- ware (already active), or the playback one has already opened the input channel which should be used by the record software.

This problem can easily be solved. First make sure that all rules for proper multi-client operation are met (not using the same record/playback channels in both programs). Then route the playback signal via TotalMix to a hardware output in the range of the record software, and activate Loop- back for recording.

Mixing several input signals into one record channel In some cases it is useful to record several sources into only one track. For example when using two microphones recording instruments and loudspeakers, TotalMix' Loopback mode saves an external mixing desk. Simply route/mix the input signals to the same output (third row), then re- define this output into a record channel via Loopback. This way any number of input channels from different sources can be recorded into one single track.

27.5 随处复制和粘贴
以上三个技巧使用混音器视图所有通道上可用的右键单击上下文菜单中的功能。这些菜单也可在矩阵中使用，但只能直接在通道标签上使用。它们是不言自明的，并会自动调整到执行单击的位置。输入通道提供清除、复制输入、粘贴输入混音和粘贴其 FX。在播放通道上，可以使用复制、粘贴和清除播放混音。在输出通道上，提供当前子混音的复制和镜像功能以及 FX 设置的复制。
这些选项是非常先进和强大的工具，可以快速完成不可能的任务。但不必担心会破坏某些东西，因为只需单击（或多次）撤消按钮即可回到开始的地方！

27.6 录制子混音 - 环回
TotalMix 包含一个内部环回功能，从硬件输出到录制软件。硬件输出的信号被发送到录制软件，而不是硬件输入的信号。这样，无需外部环回电缆即可录制子混音。此外，一个软件的回放也可以由另一个软件录制。
该功能由硬件输出的设置面板中的环回按钮激活。
在环回模式下，相应通道的硬件输入处的信号不再发送到录音软件，但仍会传递到 TotalMix。因此，TotalMix 可用于将此输入信号路由到任何硬件输出。使用子组录音，输入仍可录制在不同的通道上。
由于 10 个立体声硬件输出中的每一个都可以路由到录音软件，并且这些硬件输入都不会丢失，因此 TotalMix 提供了任何其他解决方案都无法比拟的整体灵活性和性能。
反馈是环回方法的基本问题，风险很低，因为反馈不会发生在混音器内，只有当音频软件切换到监控模式时才会发生。

框图显示了软件的输入信号如何播放，以及如何从硬件输出反馈到软件输入。

框图还显示了为什么在激活 Loopback 后，硬件输出的所有 PEQ 等现在都在录音路径内。在激活 Loopback 后，输入的激活 PEQ 和 Dyn 不在录音路径中，仅在监控路径中，即使激活了用于录音的选项 DSP – EQ+D 也是如此。

录制软件的播放

在实际应用中，使用另一个软件录制软件的输出会出现以下问题：录音软件尝试打开与播放软件（已激活）相同的播放通道，或者播放软件已经打开了录音软件应使用的输入通道。

这个问题很容易解决。首先确保满足正确的多客户端操作的所有规则（不在两个程序中使用相同的录音/播放通道）。然后通过 TotalMix 将播放信号路由到录音软件范围内的硬件输出，并激活 Loopback 进行录音。

将多个输入信号混合到一个录音通道 在某些情况下，将多个源录制到一个音轨中很有用。例如，当使用两个麦克风录制乐器和扬声器时，TotalMix 的 Loopback 模式可节省外部混音台。只需将输入信号路由/混合到同一输出（第三行），然后通过 Loopback 将此输出重新定义为录制通道。这样，来自不同来源的任意数量的输入通道都可以录制到单个音轨中。

27.7 MS Processing
The mid/side principle is a special positioning technique for micro- phones, which results in a mid signal on one channel and a side signal on the other channel. This information can be transformed back into a stereo signal quite easily. The process sends the mon- aural mid channel to left and right, the side channel too, but phase inverted (180°) to the right channel. For a better understanding: the mid channel represents the function L+R, while the side channel represents L-R.

During record the monitoring needs to be done in 'conventional' stereo. Therefore TotalMix also offers the functionality of a M/S-decoder. Activation is done in the Settings panel of the Hardware Input and Software Playback channels via the MS Proc button.

The M/S-Processing automatically operates as M/S encoder or decoder, depending on the source signal format. When processing a usual stereo signal, all monaural information will be shifted into the left channel, all stereo information into the right channel. Thus the stereo signal is M/S en- coded. This yields some interesting insights into the mono/stereo contents of modern music pro- ductions. Additionally some very interesting methods of manipulating the stereo base and gener- ating stereo effects come up, as it is then very easy to process the side channel with Low Cut, Expander, Compressor or Delay.

The most basic application is the manipulation of the stereo width: a change of the level of the side channel allows to manipulate the stereo width from mono to stereo up to extended.

27.7 MS 处理
中/侧原理是一种特殊的麦克风定位技术，可在一个通道上产生中信号，在另一个通道上产生侧信号。此信息可以很容易地转换回立体声信号。该过程将单声道中声道发送到左声道和右声道，侧声道也是如此，但相位反转（180°）到右声道。为了更好地理解：中声道代表功能 L+R，而侧声道代表 L-R。

录制期间，需要以“传统”立体声进行监控。因此，TotalMix 还提供 M/S 解码器的功能。通过 MS Proc 按钮在“硬件输入”和“软件播放”通道的“设置”面板中完成激活。

M/S 处理自动作为 M/S 编码器或解码器运行，具体取决于源信号格式。处理通常的立体声信号时，所有单声道信息将移至左声道，所有立体声信息将移至右声道。因此立体声信号是 M/S 编码的。这为现代音乐制作的单声道/立体声内容提供了一些有趣的见解。此外，还出现了一些非常有趣的方法来操纵立体声基座和产生立体声效果，因为这样就可以非常轻松地使用低切、扩展器、压缩器或延迟来处理侧声道。

最基本的应用是操纵立体声宽度：改变侧声道的级别可以操纵立体声宽度从单声道到立体声再到扩展。

27.8 Program start options (Windows only)
Under Windows TotalMix FX supports command line options. Appending the file name of a work- space loads it. Example: TotalMixFX.exe path\startworkspace.tmws. For automated loading, the parameter /nc can be used to suppress the confirmation dialog of loading the workspace.

Class Compliant Mode

31. General
The Fireface UCX II operates in two different modes: driver-based USB2 and Class Compliant.
The latter describes a standard that is natively supported by operating systems like Windows, Mac OS X and Linux. No proprietary drivers are required, the device will be directly recognized when the CC firmware is loaded. The natively available features will be limited in comparison to those provided by the RME driver for the UCX II. For example there will be no hardware settings, no (Total) Mix and no settings for the effects. However, on the iPad (iOS) the UCX II can be fully controlled by the optional app TotalMix FX for iPad.

The Class Compliant mode can be activated and deactivated directly at the unit, as long as it is not connected to a Mac or PC: SETUP/REV, Options, Hardware/Diagnosis, CC Mode On.

Neither Windows nor Mac Class Compliant support is relevant, though, since there are dedicated and matured drivers for both OS X and Windows, which provide ALL the device's features at lowest latencies. The main reason for implementing Class Compliant mode was to allow connect- ing the UCX II to an Apple iPad ® !

The UCX II provides the iPad with the professional analog I/O connections it lacks. Superb mi- crophone preamps with EQ, dynamics, even with the AutoSet feature, in addition to professional balanced line outputs, and hi-power headphone outputs which also excel with high impedance phones. Plus level and gain adjustments, AES and ADAT I/O, reverb, echo, zero latency moni- toring, digitally via USB, without any limitation in quality, at up to 96 kHz and 24 bit. And of course a Sysex-capable MIDI I/O.

DURec, the direct USB recording on USB memory stick or portable external hard drive, is also
available in Class Compliant mode. Especially in regard to recording on the iPad it is not only a world-wide unique and extraordinary feature, it also proves to be something you don’t want to miss in real-world usage.

TotalMix FX for iPad also provides full support for the optional ARC USB, an Advanced Remote Control specifically for the UCX II, making operation and handling of the iPad DAW even more easy and comfortable.

Press the encoder again to change back to the knob symbol, then turn the encoder to select On. The unit now reboots. After that the field USB in the level meter screen shows CC.

Below CC Mode you’ll find the option CC Mix, which offers different modes and routings of the playback signals. See chapter 36.

Connect the USB cable to the UCX II and the Lightning to USB adapter. Start the iPhone/iPad and plug-in the adapter. If everything works as expected, the unit will enter CC host mode, indi- cated by the yellow CC field. Audio playback will automatically be performed by the UCX II, using analog outputs 1/2 and the phones outputs 7/8 (with routing option 8 Ch. + Phones activated).

33.2 Class Compliant Mode under Windows and Mac OS X
The Class Compliant mode of the UCX II is not compatible to Windows. The UCX II will be de- tected, but automatic driver in- stallation will fail.

Mac OS X can be used in Class Compliant mode, although with- out Settings dialog and TotalMix FX it makes not much sense to do so. At least one can use the Audio MIDI Setup to check the available CC modes.

The Audio window in the Audio MIDI Setup lists four modes: 8, 14, 16 and 20 channels. The sample rate shown is the one set in the UCX II.

With 8 and 14 channels chosen up to 192 kHz sample rate can be used. Under iOS 192 kHz is not possible, as there exists no way to select the second or further Alternate Setting on the record side. iOS and all apps always use the first setting, 20 channels, which limits the sample rate for record and playback to 96 kHz.

33.2 Windows 和 Mac OS X 下的类兼容模式
UCX II 的类兼容模式与 Windows 不兼容。UCX II 将被检测到，但自动驱动程序安装将失败。

Mac OS X 可以在类兼容模式下使用，尽管没有设置对话框和 TotalMix FX，这样做没有多大意义。至少可以使用音频 MIDI 设置来检查可用的 CC 模式。

音频 MIDI 设置中的音频窗口列出了四种模式：8、14、16 和 20 通道。显示的采样率是 UCX II 中设置的采样率。

选择 8 和 14 通道时，最多可以使用 192 kHz 的采样率。在 iOS 下 192 kHz 是不可能的，因为没有办法在录制方面选择第二个或更多的备用设置。iOS 和所有应用程序始终使用第一个设置，即 20 个通道，这将录制和播放的采样率限制为 96 kHz。

34. Supported Inputs and Outputs
When connected to an iPhone/iPad, the analog mic/line input 1 works with mono apps, inputs 1 and 2 with stereo apps (both dual mono and stereo), and up to 8 inputs with 8-channel applica- tions like MultiTrack DAW and Music Studio. Garage Band supports all inputs, but only two at a time. Auria and Cubasis can record all 20 inputs simultaneously.

Playback will use the rear-side analog outputs 1 and 2, or even more channels if the app supports such operation. The UCX II can optionally copy the playback channels 1/2 to outputs 7/8, SPDIF and ADAT 1/2 for easier monitoring (CC Mix: 8-Channel + Phones). The current routing can easily be checked and verified with the level meter display. All outputs can be processed independently (volume, EQ, dynamics, FX return).

Configuring the UCX II is ideally done via the app TotalMix FX for iPad, otherwise by loading Setups. These are written into the unit while operated with PC/Mac via TotalMix FX, also storing control clock mode master or AutoSync and the current sample rate.

Using RME’s iPad app the Mix modes are automatically disabled as all routing is now controlled by the app.

The UCX II (and with it the iPhone/iPad) will be synchronized to an external digital sample rate if there is a valid digital input signal. With a wrong sample rate heavy audio noise will occur. Without an AES/SPDIF or ADAT input signal the UCX II enters master mode and uses the sample rate set by Mac OS X or iOS (the app in use).

While the MIDI I/O will send and receive Sysex messages, not all apps are ready to do this. The app Midi Tool Box can be used to verify that the UCX II is working correctly.

34. 支持的输入和输出
连接到 iPhone/iPad 时，模拟麦克风/线路输入 1 可与单声道应用程序配合使用，输入 1 和 2 可与立体声应用程序配合使用（双单声道和立体声），最多 8 个输入可与 8 通道应用程序（如 MultiTrack DAW 和 Music Studio）配合使用。Garage Band 支持所有输入，但一次只能支持两个。Auria 和 Cubasis 可以同时录制所有 20 个输入。

播放将使用后侧模拟输出 1 和 2，如果应用程序支持此类操作，则可以使用更多通道。UCX II 可以选择性地将播放通道 1/2 复制到输出 7/8、SPDIF 和 ADAT 1/2，以便于监控（CC Mix：8 通道 + 耳机）。可以使用电平表显示轻松检查和验证当前​​路由。所有输出都可以独立处理（音量、均衡器、动态、FX 返回）。

UCX II 的配置最好通过 iPad 版 TotalMix FX 应用程序完成，否则可通过加载设置完成。这些内容在通过 TotalMix FX 使用 PC/Mac 操作时写入设备，同时存储控制时钟模式主控或 AutoSync 和当前采样率。

使用 RME 的 iPad 应用程序，混音模式将自动禁用，因为所有路由现在均由应用程序控制。

如果有有效的数字输入信号，UCX II（以及 iPhone/iPad）将同步到外部数字采样率。如果采样率错误，将产生严重的音频噪音。如果没有 AES/SPDIF 或 ADAT 输入信号，UCX II 将进入主模式并使用 Mac OS X 或 iOS（正在使用的应用程序）设置的采样率。

虽然 MIDI I/O 将发送和接收 Sysex 消息，但并非所有应用程序都准备好执行此操作。应用程序 Midi Tool Box 可用于验证 UCX II 是否正常工作。

35. Front panel operation
The front panel operation is identical to the one under Windows and OS X, or standalone opera- tion. The display therefore gives the usual easy access to all features and offers a quick editing of: input gains, playback levels, routing and monitoring, echo, reverb, EQ, dynamics, sample rate, status of the optical output (ADAT/SPDIF), and much more.

36. Audio Routing and Processing
In Class Compliant mode, the UCX II features operation very similar to normal operation with
TotalMix FX, with two major differences:
▶ There is no second TotalMix row (no software playback channels), hence no individual routing or mixing of playback channels (unless the optional app TotalMix FX for iPad is used). These will always be connected either straight to the physical outputs (CC Mix 20 Ch.), or have an additional signal copy, see below.
▶ The input effects EQ/Dyn will always be active in the recording path

The UCX II can be pre-configured for CC mode via TotalMix FX under Windows/Mac by saving up to 6 configurations into its setup memory, see next chapter.

The input signal will pass through all activated functions of the TM FX input channel, namely settings, EQ, and Dynamics, and is then sent to the iPhone/iPad. Like under Windows and Mac, the input fader setting controls direct monitoring to the specified outputs. The FX send control for echo/reverb is also active.

！ As in normal TotalMix FX use, the options Stereo Width, MS Proc and Phase L/R are NOT within the recording path, but in the monitor path. This is technically determined and can not be changed.

The iPhone/iPad output signal passes through all activated functions of the TM FX hardware outputs, namely settings, EQ, and dynamics. Configuring the Phones output is done by output channels 7/8.

35. 前面板操作
前面板操作与 Windows 和 OS X 下的操作相同，或独立操作。因此，显示屏可以轻松访问所有功能，并可以快速编辑：输入增益、播放级别、路由和监控、回声、混响、EQ、动态、采样率、光纤输出状态（ADAT/SPDIF）等等。

36. 音频路由和处理
在 Class Compliant 模式下，UCX II 的操作与 TotalMix FX 的正常操作非常相似，但有两个主要区别：
▶ 没有第二个 TotalMix 行（没有软件播放通道），因此没有单独的路由或播放通道混合（除非使用可选应用程序 TotalMix FX for iPad）。这些将始终直接连接到物理输出（CC Mix 20 Ch.），或具有额外的信号副本，见下文。
▶ 输入效果 EQ/Dyn 将始终在录音路径中处于活动状态

UCX II 可以通过 Windows/Mac 下的 TotalMix FX 预先配置为 CC 模式，方法是将最多 6 个配置保存到其设置内存中，请参阅下一章。

输入信号将通过 TM FX 输入通道的所有激活功能，即设置、EQ 和动态，然后发送到 iPhone/iPad。与在 Windows 和 Mac 下一样，输入推子设置控制对指定输出的直接监控。回声/混响的 FX 发送控制也处于活动状态。

！与正常的 TotalMix FX 使用一样，立体声宽度、MS Proc 和相位 L/R 选项不在录音路径内，而是在监控路径中。这是技术上确定的，无法更改。

iPhone/iPad 输出信号通过 TM FX 硬件输出的所有激活功能，即设置、EQ 和动态。通过输出通道 7/8 配置手机输出。

CC Mix Modes
The CC Mix modes can be activated at any time and do not influence the mixer state loaded via the Setups, as Mix modes only influence the playback routing. The current routing can easily be checked and verified with the level meter display.

The unit offers four different Mix modes to ease using the CC mode without TotalMix FX for iPad.

They copy playback signals to various channels.

TotalMix App. Locks various settings in several menus to avoid conflicts with TotalMix FX for iPad, in case the app is not in background mode. Note that all copy modes are automatically disabled when the TotalMix app is detected.

8 Ch. + Phones. 8 channels of playback. Channels 1/2 are copied to Phones, SPDIF and ADAT 1/2. Channels 3/4 are copied to AES and ADAT 3/4, channels 5/6 and 7/8 to ADAT 5/6 and 7/8.

8 Ch. Playback. Same, but no copy of 1/2 to Phones, so the output Phones (7/8) is available for separate playback.

20 Ch. Playback. No copy mode active, all 20 channels are available separately.

CC 混音模式
CC 混音模式可随时激活，不会影响通过设置加载的混音器状态，因为混音模式仅影响播放路由。当前路由可通过电平表显示轻松检查和验证。

该设备提供四种不同的混音模式，以便在没有 TotalMix FX for iPad 的情况下轻松使用 CC 模式。

它们将播放信号复制到各个频道。

TotalMix 应用程序。锁定多个菜单中的各种设置，以避免与 TotalMix FX for iPad 发生冲突，以防应用程序未处于后台模式。请注意，检测到 TotalMix 应用程序时，所有复制模式都会自动禁用。

8 通道 + 手机。8 个播放通道。通道 1/2 复制到手机、SPDIF 和 ADAT 1/2。通道 3/4 复制到 AES 和 ADAT 3/4，通道 5/6 和 7/8 复制​​到 ADAT 5/6 和 7/8。

8 通道播放。相同，但没有将 1/2 复制到 Phones，因此输出 Phones (7/8) 可供单独播放。

20 通道播放。未激活复制模式，所有 20 个通道均可单独使用。

37. Setups
TotalMix FX can transfer all current settings to the device (Options/Store Current State into Device). There are six memory slots (Setups) available. An example:
▶ Inputs with no processing, gain set to +30 dB and phantom power active. Outputs without processing, faders for 1/2 set to 0 dB, 9/10 at – 20 dB, clock mode master, saved in Setup 1.
▶ Inputs with Compressor, Factory Preset Mix Limiter (digital overload protection), gain set to +30 dB, no phantom power, outputs 1/2 with EQ (factory preset Modern Bass Treble), outputs 9/10 with EQ (factory preset Cheap Phones Compensation), faders for 1/2 at 0 dB, 9/10 at - 20dB, clock mode AutoSync, saved in Setup 2.

Via the menu SETUP/REV, Setups, one can switch between setups 1 and 2 at any time, alternat- ing between entirely different configurations. Further examples would be activating reverb/echo, or temporary monitoring of the input signal. Very comfortable is the use of the Advanced Remote USB, as its buttons can load the Setups 1 to 6 directly

On the iPad the optional app TotalMix FX for iPad gives full control of all functions and parameters in real-time and with full overview, similar to working under Windows and Mac.

37. 设置
TotalMix FX 可以将所有当前设置传输到设备（选项/将当前状态存储到设备）。有六个内存插槽（设置）可用。示例：
▶ 输入无处理，增益设置为 +30 dB，幻象电源处于活动状态。输出未经过处理，推子 1/2 设置为 0 dB，9/10 设置为 - 20 dB，时钟模式为主，保存在设置 1 中。
▶ 输入带压缩器，出厂预设混音限制器（数字过载保护），增益设置为 +30 dB，无幻象电源，输出 1/2 带 EQ（出厂预设现代低音高音），输出 9/10 带 EQ（出厂预设廉价手机补偿），推子 1/2 设置为 0 dB，9/10 设置为 - 20dB，时钟模式 AutoSync，保存在设置 2 中。

通过菜单 SETUP/REV、Setup，可以随时在设置 1 和 2 之间切换，在完全不同的配置之间交替。其他示例包括激活混响/回声，或临时监控输入信号。使用 Advanced Remote USB 非常方便，因为它的按钮可以直接加载设置 1 至 6

在 iPad 上，可选应用程序 TotalMix FX for iPad 可以实时全面控制所有功能和参数，并提供完整的概览，类似于在 Windows 和 Mac 下工作。
