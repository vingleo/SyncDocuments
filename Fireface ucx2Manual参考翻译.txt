Safety Instructions and Proper use

Read the manual carefully and completely before using the device. Pay attention to the following information on how to use and operate the Fireface UCX II safely. Improper use can lead to loss of warranty claims (see warranty statement on page 82).

Proper Use
The Fireface UCX II is a digital interface for professional audio applications for use with CE ap- proved class B computers.

To comply with the European CE standard, the Fireface UCX II must be used with CE approved Class B computers. All connecting cables must be shielded.

The computer and all cables connected to the Fireface UCX II must be properly grounded. Operation with non-certified computers and cables may cause inter- ference to other devices as well as the Fireface UCX II.

Do not open chassis
No user serviceable parts inside. Refer service to qualified service personnel.

To reduce the risk of fire or electric shock do not expose this device to rain or moisture. Prevent moisture and water from entering the device. Never leave a pot with liquid on top of the device. Do not use this product near water, i. e. swimming pool, bathtub or wet basement. Danger of condensation inside – don't turn on before the device has reached room temperature.

Installation
Surface may become hot during operation – ensure sufficient ventilation.
Avoid direct sun light and do not place it near other sources of heat, like radi- ators or stoves. When mounting in a rack, leave some space between this device and others for ventilation.

Unauthorized servicing/repair voids warranty. Only use accessories specified by the manufacturer.

5. First Usage – Quick Start
5.1 Connectors – Controls – Display

The front of the Fireface UCX II features two instrument and microphone inputs, a stereo headphone output, a rotary encoder with push functionality, four buttons, a graphical colour display and two status LEDs.

The Neutrik combo sockets of the two Mic/Line inputs provide XLR and 6.3 mm / 1/4" TRS connection. They have LEDs for phantom power (48V) indication.

Inputs 3/4, INST/LINE, accept both a balanced line signal as well as an unbalanced instrument signal via 1/4" TRS plug.

The analog outputs 7 and 8 feed the headphones output Phones. This low impedance output of highest quality is able to drive headphones at higher levels undistorted, no matter if low or high impedance headphones are used.

5. 首次使用 – 快速入门
5.1 连接器 – 控件 – 显示屏

Fireface UCX II 的正面有两个乐器和麦克风输入、一个立体声耳机输出、一个带按压功能的旋转编码器、四个按钮、一个图形彩色显示屏和两个状态 LED。

The four keys and the encoder, the high-resolution and clear col- our display, and a well thought- out menu structure enable the user to quickly change and configure the device’s settings completely without a computer. 5.2 Quick Start
After the driver installation (chapter 6 / 13) connect the TRS jacks or the XLR inputs with the analog signal source. The input sensitivity of the rear inputs can be changed in TotalMix (Input Channel Settings, +13 dBu / +19 dBu and Gain), assuring the highest signal to noise ratio will be achieved. Also try to achieve an optimum input level by adjusting the source itself. Raise the source’s output level until the peak level meters in TotalMix reach about –3 dB.
5.2 快速启动
安装驱动程序后（第 6/13 章），将 TRS 插孔或 XLR 输入与模拟信号源连接。后置输入的输入灵敏度可在 TotalMix 中更改（输入通道设置、+13 dBu / +19 dBu 和增益），确保实现最高的信噪比。还可以尝试通过调整源本身来实现最佳输入电平。提高源的输出电平，直到 TotalMix 中的峰值电平表达到约 -3 dB。

The signal level of the front inputs can also be optimized directly at the Fireface. The key MIC/GAIN gives direct access to this setting, which is then controlled by the encoder VALUE.

前置输入的信号电平也可以直接在 Fireface 上进行优化。MIC/GAIN 键可直接访问此设置，然后由编码器 VALUE 进行控制。

The digital outputs of the Fireface UCX II provide AES/EBU, SPDIF coaxial, SPDIF optical and ADAT optical at the respective ports.

Fireface UCX II 的数字输出在相应的端口提供 AES/EBU、SPDIF 同轴、SPDIF 光纤和 ADAT 光纤。

On the analog playback side (the DA side), a coarse adjustment of the analog output level at the rear jacks is again available in TotalMix (Out- put Channel Settings, Level), or the Channel Settings directly at the device. The big encoder VALUE controls the Main Out volume (de- fault: Line Out 1/2).

在模拟播放端（DA 端），后插孔的模拟输出电平的粗调同样可以在 TotalMix（输出通道设置、电平）或设备上的通道设置中进行。大编码器 VALUE 控制主输出音量（默认：线路输出 1/2）。

The output signal of channels 7/8, Phones, can also be controlled directly from the VALUE encoder.

通道 7/8、耳机的输出信号也可以直接从 VALUE 编码器控制。

The Fireface UCX II can store and load its current state in 6 different memory slots, called Setups (SETUP/REV, Options, Load/Store all Settings). With this, the Fireface UCX II can be used stand-alone after setting it up accordingly, replacing lots of dedicated devices (see chapter 24).

Fireface UCX II 可以在 6 个不同的内存插槽中存储和加载其当前状态，称为设置（SETUP/REV、选项、加载/存储所有设置）。这样，Fireface UCX II 可以在进行相应设置后独立使用，取代许多专用设备（参见第 24 章）。

In online mode some settings are greyed out, as these should be changed only at the computer, in the Settings dialog or in TotalMix FX. These include the choice of sample rate and the mix settings.

在线模式下，某些设置会变灰，因为这些设置只能在计算机上、在“设置”对话框或 TotalMix FX 中更改。这些设置包括采样率的选择和混音设置。

5.3 Menu and Navigation

The Fireface UCX II has a simple and clear menu structure, for fast and efficient operation directly at the device. In most cases, however, all settings will be done via the Settings dialog and TotalMix FX of the host computer. The operation on the device is typically limited to a direct adjustment of the monitoring volume (loudspeaker and headphones), the microphone gain, and DURec (Direct USB Recording). In stand-alone mode, all settings are available directly on the device.

6. Hardware, Driver and Firmware Installation
6.1 Hardware and Driver Installation

To simplify installation it is recommended to first install the drivers before the unit is connected to the computer. But it will also work the other way round.

为了简化安装，建议先安装驱动程序，然后再将设备连接到计算机。但反过来也可以。

RME is constantly improving the drivers. Please download the latest drivers from the RME website via http://rme.to/downloads. Unzip the downloaded file and start the driver installation with rmeinstaller.exe.

RME 不断改进驱动程序。请通过 RME 网站 http://rme.to/downloads 下载最新驱动程序。解压下载的文件并使用 rmeinstaller.exe 开始安装驱动程序。

Start rmeinstaller.exe and follow the instructions of the installer. After installation connect computer and UCX II using a USB 2 cable. Windows detects the new hardware as Fireface UCX II and installs the drivers automatically.

启动 rmeinstaller.exe 并按照安装程序的说明进行操作。安装后，使用 USB 2 电缆连接计算机和 UCX II。Windows 检测到新硬件为 Fireface UCX II 并自动安装驱动程序。

After a reboot, the icons of TotalMix FX and Settings dialog appear in the notification area. If not a click on the chevron leads to the settings that control the icon display.

重启后，TotalMix FX 和“设置”对话框的图标会出现在通知区域中。如果没有，单击 V 形图标将进入控制图标显示的设置。

Driver Updates do not require to remove the existing drivers. Simply install the new driver over the existing one.

驱动程序更新不需要删除现有的驱动程序。只需在现有驱动程序上安装新驱动程序即可。

6.2 De-Installing the Drivers

6.2 卸载驱动程序

8.3 Channel Count under WDM
The Fireface ADAT optical ports support sample rates of up to 192 kHz. For this to work single- channel data is spread to two or four ADAT channels using the Sample Multiplexing technique.
This reduces the number of available ADAT channels from 8 to 4 or 2 per ADAT port.

8.3 WDM 下的通道数
Fireface ADAT 光纤端口支持高达 192 kHz 的采样率。为此，使用采样复用技术将单通道数据扩展到两个或四个 ADAT 通道。
这会将每个 ADAT 端口可用的 ADAT 通道数从 8 个减少到 4 个或 2 个。

Whenever the Fireface changes into Double Speed (88.2/96 kHz) or Quad Speed mode (176.4/192 kHz) all devices no longer available vanish automatically.

每当 Fireface 变为双倍速度（88.2/96 kHz）或四倍速度模式（176.4/192 kHz）时，所有不再可用的设备都会自动消失。

8.4 Multi-client Operation
RME audio interfaces support multi-client operation. Several programs can be used at the same time. The formats ASIO and WDM can even be used on the same playback channels simultane- ously. But as WDM uses a real-time sample rate conversion (ASIO does not), all active ASIO software has to use the same sample rate.

8.4 多客户端操作
RME 音频接口支持多客户端操作。可同时使用多个程序。ASIO 和 WDM 格式甚至可以同时在相同的播放通道上使用。但由于 WDM 使用实时采样率转换（ASIO 不使用），因此所有活动的 ASIO 软件都必须使用相同的采样率。

However, a better overview is maintained by using the channels exclusively. This is no limitation at all, because TotalMix allows for any output routing, and therefore a playback of multiple soft- ware on the same hardware outputs.

但是，通过专门使用通道可以保持更好的概览。这完全没有限制，因为 TotalMix 允许任何输出路由，因此可以在相同的硬件输出上播放多个软件。

Inputs can be used from an unlimited number of WDM and ASIO software at the same time, as the driver simply sends the data to all applications simultaneously.

由于驱动程序只需将数据同时发送到所有应用程序，因此可以同时使用来自无限数量的 WDM 和 ASIO 软件的输入。

RME's sophisticated tool DIGICheck is an exception to this rule. It operates like an ASIO host,
using a special technique to access playback channels directly. Therefore DIGICheck is able to analyse and display playback data from any software, no matter which format it uses.

RME 的复杂工具 DIGICheck 是这一规则的一个例外。它的运行方式类似于 ASIO 主机，
使用特殊技术直接访问播放通道。因此，无论软件使用哪种格式，DIGICheck 都能够分析和显示来自任何软件的播放数据。

8.5 Analog Recording
For recordings via the analog inputs the corresponding record device has to be chosen (Fireface UCX II Analog (x+x)).

8.5 模拟录音
对于通过模拟输入进行录音，必须选择相应的录音设备（Fireface UCX II Analog (x+x)）。

The input sensitivity of the rear inputs can be changed in two steps and with adjustable gain in TotalMix (Input Channel Settings, Level), assuring the highest signal to noise ratio will be achieved. A further optimization can be achieved by adjusting the source itself. Raise the source’s output level until the peak level meters in TotalMix reach about –3 dB.

后置输入的输入灵敏度可以分两步更改，TotalMix 中的增益可调（输入通道设置、电平），确保实现最高的信噪比。通过调整源本身可以实现进一步的优化。提高源的输出电平，直到 TotalMix 中的峰值电平表达到约 -3 dB。

The level of the front-side analog inputs can be optimized via TotalMix (Input Channel Settings, Gain), or directly at the Fireface UCX II by the key Mic/Gain and the encoders 1/2. The multi- colour level meters shown on the display also provide useful information about the current level state.

可以通过 TotalMix（输入通道设置、增益）或直接在 Fireface UCX II 上通过按键 Mic/Gain 和编码器 1/2 优化前端模拟输入的电平。显示屏上显示的多色电平表还提供有关当前电平状态的有用信息。

Further information is found in chapter 19.
更多信息请参见第 19 章。

It often makes sense to monitor the input signal or send it directly to the output. This can be done at zero latency using TotalMix FX (see chapter 25).

监控输入信号或将其直接发送到输出通常很有意义。这可以使用 TotalMix FX 以零延迟完成（参见第 25 章）。

An automated control of real-time monitoring can be achieved by Steinberg’s ASIO protocol with RME’s ASIO drivers and all ASIO 2.0 compatible programs. When 'ASIO Direct Monitoring' has been switched on, the input signal is routed in real-time to the output whenever a recording is started (punch-in).

Steinberg 的 ASIO 协议与 RME 的 ASIO 驱动程序以及所有 ASIO 2.0 兼容程序可实现实时监控的自动控制。打开“ASIO 直接监控”后，只要开始录音（插入），输入信号就会实时路由到输出。

8.6 Digital Recording
Unlike analog soundcards which produce empty wave files (or noise) when no input signal is present, digital interfaces always need a valid input signal to start recording.

8.6 数字录音
与模拟声卡不同，模拟声卡在没有输入信号时会产生空的波形文件（或噪音），而数字接口始终需要有效的输入信号才能开始录音。

Taking this into account, RME added a compre- hensive I/O signal status display to the Fireface UCX II, showing sample frequency, lock and sync status for every input, and several statusfields in the unit’s display.

考虑到这一点，RME 为 Fireface UCX II 添加了全面的 I/O 信号状态显示，显示每个输入的采样频率、锁定和同步状态、以及设备显示屏中的多个状态字段。

The sample frequency shown in the fields Clock Mode and Input Status of the Settings dialog is useful as a quick display of the current configura- tion of the unit and the connected external equip- ment. If no sample frequency is recognized, it will read ‘No Lock’.

设置对话框中的时钟模式和输入状态字段中显示的采样频率有助于快速显示设备和所连接外部设备的当前配置。如果未识别采样频率，则会显示“无锁定”。

This way, configuring any suitable audio application for digital recording is simple. After selecting the correct input, Fireface UCX II displays the current sample frequency. This parameter can then be changed in the application’s audio attributes (or similar) dialog.

这样，配置任何适合数字录音的音频应用程序都很简单。选择正确的输入后，Fireface UCX II 将显示当前采样频率。然后可以在应用程序的音频属性（或类似）对话框中更改此参数。

8.7 Clock Modes - Synchronization
In the digital world, all devices must be either Master (clock source) or Slave (clock receiver).
Whenever several devices are linked within a system, there must always be a single master clock.

8.7 时钟模式 - 同步
在数字世界中，所有设备必须是主设备（时钟源）或从设备（时钟接收器）。
每当系统中连接多个设备时，必须始终有一个主时钟。

! A digital system can only have one master! If the Fireface’s clock mode is set to 'Master', all other devices must be set to ‘Slave’.

！数字系统只能有一个主设备！如果 Fireface 的时钟模式设置为“主设备”，则所有其他设备必须设置为“从设备”。

The Fireface UCX II utilizes a very user-friendly, intelligent clock control, called AutoSync. In AutoSync mode, the system constantly scans the digital input for a valid signal. If any valid signal is found, the Fireface switches from the internal quartz (Clock Source – Current Internal) to a clock extracted from the input signal (Clock Source – Current ADAT, SPDIF, AES or Word). The difference to a usual slave mode is that whenever the clock reference fails, the system will auto- matically use its internal clock and operate in clock mode Master.

Fireface UCX II 采用非常用户友好的智能时钟控制，称为 AutoSync。在 AutoSync 模式下，系统不断扫描数字输入以查找有效信号。如果发现任何有效信号，Fireface 将从内部石英（时钟源 - 当前内部）切换到从输入信号中提取的时钟（时钟源 - 当前 ADAT、SPDIF、AES 或 Word）。与通常的从属模式的区别在于，每当时钟参考失败时，系统将自动使用其内部时钟并在主时钟模式下运行。

AutoSync guarantees that record and record-while-play will always work correctly. In certain cases however, AutoSync may cause feedback in the digital carrier, so synchronization breaks down. To remedy this, switch the Fireface clock mode to ‘Internal’.

AutoSync 可确保录制和边录边放功能始终正常工作。但在某些情况下，AutoSync 可能会导致数字载体出现反馈，从而导致同步中断。要解决此问题，请将 Fireface 时钟模式切换为“内部”。

RME’s exclusive SyncCheck technology enables an easy to use check and display of the current clock status. SyncCheck indicates whether there is a valid signal (Lock, No Lock) for each input (Word Clock, SPDIF, ADAT, AES), or if there is a valid and synchronous signal (Sync). See chap- ter 40.1.

RME 独有的 SyncCheck 技术可以轻松检查和显示当前时钟状态。SyncCheck 指示每个输入（字时钟、SPDIF、ADAT、AES）是否有有效信号（锁定、无锁定），或者是否有有效同步信号（同步）。请参阅第 40.1 章。

Via Clock Source a preferred input can be defined. As long as the Fireface sees a valid signal there, this input will be designated as the sync source, otherwise the other inputs will be scanned in turn. If none of the inputs are receiving a valid signal, the Fireface automatically switches clock mode to ‘Internal’.

通过时钟源可以定义首选输入。只要 Fireface 看到有效信号，此输入将被指定为同步源，否则将依次扫描其他输入。如果没有输入接收到有效信号，Fireface 会自动将时钟模式切换为“内部”。

Under WDM the Fireface will (has to) set the sample rate. Therefore the error shown to the right can occur. An AES, SPDIF or ADAT signal with a sample rate of 48 kHz is used as sync source, but Windows audio had been set to 44100 Hz before. The red colored text label signals the error condition, and prompts the user to set 48000 Hz manually as sample rate.

在 WDM 下，Fireface 将（必须）设置采样率。因此可能会出现右侧所示的错误。采样率为 48 kHz 的 AES、SPDIF 或 ADAT 信号用作同步源，但 Windows 音频之前已设置为 44100 Hz。红色文本标签表示错误情况，并提示用户手动设置 48000 Hz 作为采样率。

Under ASIO the audio software sets the sample rate, so that such an error will usually not happen – but it can too. In slave mode the external sample rate has priority. Feeding 44.1 kHz will prevent the ASIO software to set 48 kHz – obviously, as the only way to do so would be to enter a different clock mode (Master/Internal).

在 ASIO 下，音频软件会设置采样率，因此通常不会发生此类错误 - 但也可能出现。在从属模式下，外部采样率具有优先权。输入 44.1 kHz 将阻止 ASIO 软件设置 48 kHz - 显然，因为这样做的唯一方法是进入不同的时钟模式（主/内部）。

In practice, SyncCheck provides the user with an easy way of checking whether all digital devices connected to the system are properly configured. With SyncCheck, finally anyone can master this common source of error, previously one of the most complex issues in the digital studio world.

实际上，SyncCheck 为用户提供了一种简单的方法来检查连接到系统的所有数字设备是否配置正确。有了 SyncCheck，终于任何人都可以掌握这个常见的错误来源，而这曾经是数字工作室领域最复杂的问题之一。

9.3 已知问题
如果计算机没有提供足够的 CPU 功率，和/或足够的 USB 或 PCI 总线传输速率，和/或足够的 PCIe 总线传输速率，则会出现中断、噼啪声和噪音。
在大多数情况下，在 Fireface UCX II 的“设置”对话框中增加缓冲区大小会有所帮助。还建议停用所有插件，以验证这些不是造成此类影响的原因。
更多信息请参见第 40.3 章。

Another common source of trouble is incorrect synchronization. ASIO does not support asynchro-nous operation, which means that the input and output signals not only have to use the same sample frequency, but also have to be in sync. All devices connected to the Fireface UCX II must be properly configured for full duplex operation. As long as SyncCheck (in the Settings dialog) only displays Lock instead of Sync, the devices have not been set up properly.

另一个常见的麻烦来源是同步不正确。 ASIO 不支持异步操作，这意味着输入和输出信号不仅必须使用相同的采样频率，还必须同步。 必须正确配置连接到 Fireface UCX II 的所有设备才能进行全双工操作。 只要 SyncCheck（在“设置”对话框中）仅显示“锁定”而不是“同步”，就表示设备设置不正确。

The same applies when using more than one Fireface UCX II - they all have to be in sync. Else a periodically repeated noise will be heard.

使用多个 Fireface UCX II 时也一样 - 它们必须同步。否则会听到周期性重复的噪音。

RME devices support ASIO Direct Monitoring (ADM). Please note that not all programs support ADM completely or error-free. The most often reported problem is the wrong behaviour of pano- rama in a stereo channel. Also try to avoid setting the TotalMix FX hardware outputs (third row) to mono mode. This will most likely break ADM compatibility.

RME 设备支持 ASIO 直接监控 (ADM)。请注意，并非所有程序都完全或无错误地支持 ADM。最常报告的问题是立体声通道中全景声的行为错误。还请尽量避免将 TotalMix FX 硬件输出（第三行）设置为单声道模式。这很可能会破坏 ADM 兼容性。

In case of a drift between audio and MIDI, or in case of a fixed deviation (MIDI notes placed close before or behind the correct position), the settings in Cubase/Nuendo have to be changed. At the time of print the option 'Use System Timestamp' should be activated. The Fireface UCX II sup- ports both MME MIDI and DirectMusic MIDI. It depends on the used application which one will work better.

如果音频和 MIDI 之间存在偏移，或者存在固定偏差（MIDI 音符放置在正确位置之前或之后），则必须更改 Cubase/Nuendo 中的设置。在打印时，应激活“使用系统时间戳”选项。Fireface UCX II 支持 MME MIDI 和 DirectMusic MIDI。这取决于使用的应用程序，哪一个会更好。

10. Using more than one Fireface UCX II
The driver supports up to three Fireface UCX II or a combination of up to three supported inter- faces. All units have to be in sync, i.e. have to receive valid sync information (either via word clock or by using AutoSync and feeding synchronized signals).

10. 使用多个 Fireface UCX II
驱动程序支持最多三个 Fireface UCX II 或最多三个受支持的接口的组合。所有设备必须同步，即必须接收有效的同步信息（通过字时钟或使用 AutoSync 并输入同步信号）。

● If one of the Firefaces is set to clock mode Master, all others have to be set to clock mode
AutoSync, and have to be synced from the master, for example by feeding word clock. The clock modes of all units have to be set up correctly in the Fireface Settings dialog.

● If all units are fed with a synchronous clock, i.e. all units show Sync in their Settings dialog, all channels can be used at once. This is especially easy to handle under ASIO, as the ASIO driver presents all units as one.

● 如果其中一个 Fireface 设置为时钟模式 Master，则所有其他 Fireface 都必须设置为时钟模式
AutoSync，并且必须与主设备同步，例如通过输入字时钟。必须在 Fireface 设置对话框中正确设置所有设备的时钟模式。

● 如果所有设备都使用同步时钟，即所有设备在其设置对话框中都显示 Sync，则可以同时使用所有通道。这在 ASIO 下尤其容易处理，因为 ASIO 驱动程序将所有设备作为一个整体呈现。


Please be aware that operating more than one Fireface UCX II may cause resource problems on
the computer side.

Note: TotalMix is part of the hardware of each Fireface. Up to three mixers are available, but these are separated and can't interchange data. Therefore a global mixer for all units is not possible.

请注意，操作多个 Fireface UCX II 可能会导致计算机端出现资源问题。

注意：TotalMix 是每个 Fireface 硬件的一部分。最多有三个混音器可用，但它们是分开的，无法交换数据。因此，无法为所有设备提供全局混音器。

20.3 DC-coupled Outputs (CV/Gate)
All eight analog outputs of the UCX II are DC-coupled. AC coupling via capacitor is common, both directly after the DAC chip and directly at the output socket. This prevents a fixed DC component at the output, which could cause negative effects such as popping noise or malfunctions in other devices that are also DC-coupled. However, it also serves as protection against dangerous DC from the outside.

20.3 直流耦合输出（CV/Gate）
UCX II 的所有八个模拟输出均为直流耦合。通过电容器的交流耦合很常见，直接在 DAC 芯片之后和直接在输出插座处进行。这可以防止输出端出现固定直流分量，这可能会导致负面影响，例如爆音或其他同样直流耦合的设备发生故障。但是，它也可以作为对来自外部的危险直流电的保护。

DC coupling has the obvious advantage of allowing the output to operate level-linearly down to 0 Hz. Even at extremely low bass (16 Hz) phase shifts are avoided. DC-coupled inputs are rare, especially for power amplifiers, so a possible DC offset of a playback normally does not cause serious issues.

直流耦合的明显优势在于允许输出以线性方式工作至 0 Hz。即使在极低的低音 (16 Hz) 下也可以避免相移。直流耦合输入很少见，尤其是对于功率放大器而言，因此播放中可能出现的直流偏移通常不会引起严重问题。

In order to realize the outputs stable and reliable (robust) as DC-coupled, the circuitry was de- signed for low DC-offsets and sufficient overvoltage protection. That this works has been proven for years, not only by RME's ADI series.

为了实现输出稳定可靠（坚固）的直流耦合，电路设计为低直流偏移和足够的过压保护。这种做法多年来已被证明有效，不仅仅是 RME 的 ADI 系列。

To be able to generate DC is quite useful in measurement technology, also in connection with audio. However, DC coupling is especially desirable in combination with analog, electronic sound generators (synthesizers). These have an input that can control the pitch as well as other func- tions via a DC voltage. Control Voltage, short CV, often also CV/Gate (gate determines tone on or off), CV Filter etc. is mostly based on the voltage range 0 to +5 Volt. If an audio interface can output such DC voltages then synthesizers can be controlled via suitable software and the analog outputs of the audio interface.

能够生成直流电在测量技术中非常有用，在音频方面也是如此。但是，直流耦合与模拟电子声音发生器（合成器）结合使用时尤其有用。这些合成器有一个输入端，可以通过直流电压控制音调和其他功能。控制电压、短 CV、通常还有 CV/Gate（门控决定音调的开启或关闭）、CV 滤波器等主要基于 0 到 +5 伏的电压范围。如果音频接口可以输出这样的直流电压，那么就可以通过合适的软件和音频接口的模拟输出来控制合成器。

The DA converters and analog output stages used in the UCX II have a very low DC drift and generate the DC voltages very reliably and reproducibly, so they are very well suited for this application.

UCX II 中使用的 DA 转换器和模拟输出级具有非常低的直流漂移，并且能够非常可靠且可重复地产生直流电压，因此非常适合此应用。

To receive signals in AES/EBU format, an adapter cable is required. Pins 2 and 3 of a fe- male XLR plug are connected individually to the two pins of a phono plug. The cable shield- ing is connected to pin 1 of the XLR and the ground contact of the phono plug.

要接收 AES/EBU 格式的信号，需要使用适配器电缆。母 XLR 插头的 2 和 3 针分别连接到唱机插头的两个针脚。电缆屏蔽层连接到 XLR 的 1 针和唱机插头的接地触点。

The optical input automatically switches to SPDIF operation when such a signal is detected. The audio information is then shown in TotalMix on the first two ADAT channels ADAT 1/2, which are channels 13 and 14.

当检测到此类信号时，光纤输入会自动切换到 SPDIF 操作。然后，音频信息显示在 TotalMix 的前两个 ADAT 通道 ADAT 1/2 上，即通道 13 和 14。

SPDIF coaxial Output
Apart from the audio data itself, digital signals in SPDIF or AES/EBU format have a header con- taining channel status information. False channel status is a common cause of malfunction. The Fireface UCX II ignores the received header and creates a totally new one for the output signal.

The output SPDIF coaxial can have the Channel Status Consumer or Professional, changed in the Settings dialog. When selecting Professional, the carrier level is also doubled for better AES/EBU compatibility.

输出 SPDIF 同轴电缆的通道状态可在“设置”对话框中更改，分别为“消费者”或“专业”。选择“专业”时，载波级别也会加倍，以实现更好的 AES/EBU 兼容性。

Operation as second AES/EBU Output
Professional AES/EBU equipment can be connected to the output SPDIF coaxial thanks to its format option Professional and a doubled output voltage. Output cables should have the same pinout as those used for input (see above), but with a male XLR plug instead of a female one.

作为第二个 AES/EBU 输出使用
专业 AES/EBU 设备可以连接到输出 SPDIF 同轴电缆，这得益于其专业格式选项和双倍输出电压。输出电缆的引脚排列应与输入电缆的引脚排列相同（见上文），但使用公 XLR 插头而不是母 XLR 插头。

SPDIF optical output
To send out SPDIF from the optical output, select the option Output Format Optical – SPDIF in the Settings dialog. The output signal in TotalMix has to be present on the ADAT channels 1/2.

SPDIF 光纤输出
要从光纤输出发送 SPDIF，请在“设置”对话框中选择“输出格式光纤 - SPDIF”选项。TotalMix 中的输出信号必须出现在 ADAT 通道 1/2 上。

！ Note that most consumer HiFi equipment (with optical or phono SPDIF inputs) will only accept signals in ‘Consumer’ format!

！请注意，大多数消费类 HiFi 设备（带有光纤或唱机 SPDIF 输入）只接受“消费者”格式的信号！

Therefore in SPDIF mode the optical output is fixed to Consumer status.

因此在 SPDIF 模式下光纤输出固定为消费者状态。

21.4 MIDI
Fireface UCX II offers one MIDI I/O via two 5-pin DIN sockets. The MIDI ports are added to the system by the driver. Using MIDI capable software, these ports can be accessed under the name UCX II MIDI Port.

21.4 MIDI
Fireface UCX II 通过两个 5 针 DIN 插座提供一个 MIDI I/O。驱动程序将 MIDI 端口添加到系统中。使用支持 MIDI 的软件，这些端口可通过名称 UCX II MIDI 端口进行访问。

The MIDI ports support multi-client operation. A MIDI input signal can be received from several programs at the same time. Even the MIDI output can be used by multiple programs simultane- ously. However, due to the limited bandwidth of MIDI, this kind of application will often show various problems.

MIDI 端口支持多客户端操作，一个 MIDI 输入信号可以同时被多个程序接收，甚至 MIDI 输出也可以同时被多个程序使用，但由于 MIDI 的带宽有限，这种应用经常会出现各种问题。

Note: The MIDI input indicator displays any kind of MIDI activity, including MIDI Clock, MTC and Active Sensing. The latter is sent by most keyboards every 0.3 seconds.

注意：MIDI 输入指示器显示任何类型的 MIDI 活动，包括 MIDI 时钟、MTC 和主动感应。大多数键盘每 0.3 秒发送一次主动感应。

22. Word Clock
22.1 Word Clock Input / Output
SteadyClock guarantees an excellent performance in all clock modes. Based on the highly effi- cient jitter suppression, the Fireface refreshes and cleans up any clock signal, and provides it as reference clock at the BNC output (see chapter 40.8).

22. 字时钟
22.1 字时钟输入/输出
SteadyClock 保证所有时钟模式下的出色性能。基于高效的抖动抑制，Fireface 可刷新和清理任何时钟信号，并将其作为 BNC 输出的参考时钟提供（参见第 40.8 章）。

The Fireface UCX II has one BNC socket with dual functionality – it can serve as word clock input OR word clock output. The current mode is set in the Settings dialog by the user.

Fireface UCX II 有一个 BNC 插座，具有双重功能 - 它可以用作字时钟输入或字时钟输出。当前模式由用户在“设置”对话框中设置。

Input Mode
The Fireface's word clock input is active when the BNC socket has has been switched to input mode in the Settings dialog, and a valid word clock signal is present. The signal at the BNC input can be Single, Double or Quad Speed, the Fireface UCX II automatically adapts to it. As soon as a valid signal is detected, the WCK field is lit, and the Settings dialog shows either Lock or Sync (see chapter 40.1).

输入模式
当 BNC 插座在“设置”对话框中切换为输入模式，并且存在有效的字时钟信号时，Fireface 的字时钟输入处于活动状态。BNC 输入端的信号可以是单速、双速或四速，Fireface UCX II 会自动适应。一旦检测到有效信号，WCK 字段就会亮起，并且“设置”对话框会显示“锁定”或“同步”（参见第 40.1 章）。

Thanks to RME's Signal Adaptation Circuit, the word clock input still works correctly even with heavily mis-shaped, dc-prone, too small or overshoot- prone signals. Thanks to automatic signal centering, 300 mV (0.3V) input level is sufficient in principle. An additional hysteresis reduces sensitivity to 1.0 V, so that over- and undershoots and high frequency disturbances don't cause a wrong trigger.

得益于 RME 的信号自适应电路，字时钟输入即使在严重失真、容易产生直流、信号过小或容易出现过冲的情况下仍能正常工作。得益于自动信号居中功能，300 mV (0.3V) 输入电平原则上就足够了。额外的滞后将灵敏度降低至 1.0 V，这样过冲和欠冲以及高频干扰就不会导致错误触发。

The Fireface UCX II word clock input can be high impedance or internally terminated (75 Ohms). The state is comfortably selected within the Settings dialog. To terminate the input check the option Word Clock In Termination under Options.

Fireface UCX II 字时钟输入可以是高阻抗或内部端接（75 欧姆）。可在“设置”对话框中轻松选择状态。要终止输入，请选中“选项”下的“字时钟输入端接”选项。

Output Mode
The same BNC socket can be used as word clock output instead of input, again configured in the Settings dialog. The word clock output is constantly active, providing the current sample frequency as word clock signal. As a result, in Master mode the provided word clock is defined by the cur- rently used software. In Slave mode the provided frequency is identical to the one present at the currently chosen clock source. When the current clock signal fails, the Fireface UCX II switches to Master mode and adjusts itself to the next, best matching frequency (44.1 kHz, 48 kHz etc.).

输出模式
相同的 BNC 插座可用作字时钟输出而不是输入，同样在“设置”对话框中进行配置。字时钟输出始终处于活动状态，提供当前采样频率作为字时钟信号。因此，在主模式下，提供的字时钟由当前使用的软件定义。在从属模式下，提供的频率与当前选择的时钟源的频率相同。当当前时钟信号失败时，Fireface UCX II 会切换到主模式并自行调整到下一个最佳匹配频率（44.1 kHz、48 kHz 等）。

Selecting Single Speed in the Settings dialog causes the output signal to always stay within the range of 32 kHz to 48 kHz. So at 96 kHz and 192 kHz sample rate, the output word clock is 48 kHz.

在“设置”对话框中选择“单速”会使输出信号始终保持在 32 kHz 至 48 kHz 的范围内。因此，在 96 kHz 和 192 kHz 采样率下，输出字时钟为 48 kHz。

Thanks to a low impedance, short circuit proof output, the Fireface delivers 4 Vpp to 75 Ohms. For wrong termination with 2 x 75 Ohms (37.5 Ohms), there are still 3.3 Vpp at the output.

由于具有低阻抗、防短路输出，Fireface 可将 4 Vpp 输出至 75 欧姆。如果错误地端接 2 x 75 欧姆（37.5 欧姆），输出端仍会有 3.3 Vpp。

22.2 Technical Description and Usage
In the analog domain one can connect any device to another device, a synchronization is not necessary. Digital audio is different. It uses a clock, the sample frequency. The signal can only be processed and transmitted when all participating devices share the same clock. If not, the signal will suffer from wrong samples, distortion, crackle sounds and drop outs.

22.2 技术说明和使用
在模拟域中，可以将任何设备连接到另一设备，无需同步。数字音频则不同。它使用时钟，即采样频率。只有当所有参与设备共享同一时钟时，才能处理和传输信号。否则，信号将遭受错误采样、失真、噼啪声和中断。

AES/EBU, SPDIF and ADAT are self-clocking, an additional word clock connection in principle isn't necessary. But when using more than one device simultaneously problems are likely to hap- pen. For example any self-clocking will not work in a loop cabling, when there is no 'master' (main clock) inside the loop. Additionally the clock of all participating devices has to be synchronous.

This is often impossible with devices limited to playback, for example CD players, as these have no SPDIF input, thus can't use the self clocking technique as clock reference.

AES/EBU、SPDIF 和 ADAT 是自时钟的，原则上不需要额外的字时钟连接。但是，当同时使用多个设备时，可能会出现问题。例如，当环路内没有“主时钟”（主时钟）时，任何自时钟都无法在环路布线中工作。此外，所有参与设备的时钟必须同步。

对于仅限于播放的设备（例如 CD 播放器），这通常是不可能的，因为它们没有 SPDIF 输入，因此不能使用自时钟技术作为时钟参考。

In a digital studio synchronisation is maintained by connecting all devices to a central sync source.

For example the mixing desk works as master and sends a reference signal, the word clock, to all other devices. Of course this will only work as long as all other devices are equipped with a word clock or sync input, thus being able to work as slave (some professional CD players indeed have a word clock input). Then all devices get the same clock and will work in every possible combination with each other.

在数字工作室中，同步是通过将所有设备连接到中央同步源来维持的。

例如，混音台作为主设备工作，并向所有其他设备发送参考信号（字时钟）。当然，这只有在所有其他设备都配备字时钟或同步输入时才有效，从而能够作为从设备工作（一些专业 CD 播放器确实有字时钟输入）。然后所有设备都会获得相同的时钟，并可以在所有可能的组合中工作。

！ A digital system can only have one master! If the Fireface's clock mode is set to 'Master', all other devices must be set to ‘Slave’.

！一个数字系统只能有一个主设备！如果 Fireface 的时钟模式设置为“主设备”，则所有其他设备必须设置为“从设备”。

But word clock is not only the 'great problem solver', it also has some disadvantages. The word clock is based on a fraction of the really needed clock. For example SPDIF: 44.1 kHz word clock (a simple square wave signal) has to be multiplied by 256 inside the device using a special PLL (to about 11.2 MHz). This signal then replaces the one from the quartz crystal. Big disadvantage: because of the high multiplication factor the reconstructed clock will have great deviations called jitter. The jitter of a word clock is typically 15 times higher as when using a quartz based clock.

但是字时钟不仅是“伟大的问题解决者”，它也有一些缺点。字时钟基于真正所需时钟的一小部分。例如 SPDIF：44.1 kHz 字时钟（简单的方波信号）必须在设备内部使用特殊 PLL 乘以 256（约 11.2 MHz）。然后，该信号取代石英晶体的信号。最大的缺点：由于倍增因子高，重建的时钟将有很大的偏差，称为抖动。字时钟的抖动通常比使用石英时钟时高 15 倍。

The actual end of these problems is offered by the SteadyClock technology of the Fireface UCX II. Combining the advantages of modern and fastest digital technology with analog filter tech- niques, re-gaining a low jitter clock signal of 22 MHz from a slow word clock of 44.1 kHz is no problem anymore. Additionally, jitter on the input signal is highly rejected, so that even in real world usage the re-gained clock signal is of highest quality.

Fireface UCX II 的 SteadyClock 技术真正解决了这些问题。结合现代和最快的数字技术与模拟滤波技术的优势，从 44.1 kHz 的慢速字时钟重新获得 22 MHz 的低抖动时钟信号不再是问题。此外，输入信号的抖动被高度抑制，因此即使在实际使用中，重新获得的时钟信号也是最高质量的。

22.3 Cabling and Termination
Word clock signals are usually distributed in the form of a network, split with BNC T-adapters and terminated with resistors. We recommend using off-the-shelf BNC cables to connect all devices, as this type of cable is used for most computer networks. You will find all the necessary compo- nents (T-adapters, terminators, cables) in most electronics and/or computer stores. The latter usually carries 50 Ohms components. The 75 Ohms components used for word clock are part of video technology (RG59).

22.3 布线和终端
字时钟信号通常以网络形式分布，用 BNC T 型适配器分离并用电阻器端接。我们建议使用现成的 BNC 电缆连接所有设备，因为这种类型的电缆用于大多数计算机网络。您可以在大多数电子和/或计算机商店中找到所有必要的组件（T 型适配器、终端器、电缆）。后者通常带有 50 欧姆组件。用于字时钟的 75 欧姆组件是视频技术 (RG59) 的一部分。

Ideally, the word clock signal is a 5 Volt square wave with the frequency of the sample rate, of which the harmonics go up to far above 500 kHz. To avoid voltage loss and reflections, both the cable itself and the terminating resistor at the end of the chain should have an impedance of 75 Ohm. If the voltage is too low, synchronization will fail. High frequency reflection effects can cause both jitter and sync failure.

理想情况下，字时钟信号是 5 伏方波，频率为采样率，其谐波远高于 500 kHz。为避免电压损失和反射，电缆本身和链末端的终端电阻都应具有 75 欧姆的阻抗。如果电压太低，同步将失败。高频反射效应可能导致抖动和同步失败。

Unfortunately there are still devices on the market which provide a word clock output that can only be called unsatisfactory. If the output breaks down to 3 Volts when terminating with 75 Ohms, you have to take into account that a device, of which the input only works from 2.8 Volts and above, does not function correctly already after 3 meter cable length. So it is not astonishing that because of the higher voltage, word clock networks are in some cases more stable and reliable if cables are not terminated at all. In some cases the 75 Ohms are built into the device and cannot be switched off. In this case the network load is often 2 x 75 Ohms, and the user is forced to buy a special word clock distributor (such a device is generally recommended for bigger studios).

不幸的是，市场上仍有一些设备提供的字时钟输出不能令人满意。如果在 75 欧姆端接时输出降至 3 伏，则必须考虑到，输入电压仅在 2.8 伏及以上时才有效的设备在 3 米电缆长度后就无法正常工作。因此，由于电压较高，如果电缆根本没有端接，字时钟网络在某些情况下会更稳定、更可靠，这并不奇怪。在某些情况下，75 欧姆内置于设备中，无法关闭。在这种情况下，网络负载通常为 2 x 75 欧姆，用户不得不购买特殊的字时钟分配器（这种设备通常建议用于较大的工作室）。

Therefore it seems of advantage to design outputs of word clock as low impedance types, but all word clock inputs as high impedance types, in order to not weaken the signal on the chain.

因此，将字时钟输出设计为低阻抗类型，但将所有字时钟输入设计为高阻抗类型似乎是有利的，以便不削弱链上的信号。

The Fireface's word clock input can be high-impedance or terminated internally, ensuring maxi- mum flexibility. If termination is necessary (e.g. because the Fireface is the last device in the chain), check the option Word Clock In Term. under Options (see chapter 7.1 / 14.1).

Fireface 的字时钟输入可以是高阻抗或内部端接，以确保最大的灵活性。如果需要端接（例如，因为 Fireface 是链中的最后一个设备），请选中选项下的“Word Clock In Term.”选项（参见第 7.1/14.1 章）。

In case the UCX II resides within a chain of devices receiving word clock, plug a T-adapter into its BNC input jack, and the cable supplying the word clock signal to one end of the adapter. Connect the free end to the next device in the chain via a further BNC cable. The last device in the chain should be terminated using another T-adapter and a 75 Ohm resistor (available as short BNC plug). Of course devices with internal termination do not need T-adaptor and terminator plug.

如果 UCX II 位于接收字时钟的设备链中，请将 T 型适配器插入其 BNC 输入插孔，并将提供字时钟信号的电缆插入适配器的一端。通过另一根 BNC 电缆将自由端连接到链中的下一个设备。链中的最后一个设备应使用另一个 T 型适配器和 75 欧姆电阻器（可用作短 BNC 插头）进行端接。当然，具有内部端接的设备不需要 T 型适配器和端接器插头。

22.4 Operation
The field WCK on the display will light up green as soon as a word clock signal is detected. To change to word clock as clock source, activate the Clock Source Word in the field Clock Mode within the Settings dialog. The status display Current changes to Word as soon as a valid signal is present at the BNC jack. This message has the same meaning as the green State field, but appears on the monitor, i.e. the user can check immediately whether a valid word clock signal is present and is currently being used.

22.4 操作
一旦检测到字时钟信号，显示屏上的 WCK 字段将亮起绿色。要将字时钟更改为时钟源，请在“设置”对话框中的“时钟模式”字段中激活“时钟源字”。一旦 BNC 插孔出现有效信号，状态显示“当前”将更改为“字”。此消息与绿色“状态”字段含义相同，但显示在显示器上，即用户可以立即检查是否存在有效的字时钟信号并且当前正在使用。

23. Operation and Usage
23.1 General
Using the rotary encoder, the four buttons and the clear colour display the Fireface UCX II can be configured and set up completely at the device. Additionally internal memory allows for the per- manent storage of six different states of the unit. Therefore the Fireface UCX II is able to operate fully stand-alone, without any connected computer. 23. 操作和使用

使用旋转编码器、四个按钮和清晰的彩色显示屏，可以在设备上完全配置和设置 Fireface UCX II。此外，内部存储器可以永久存储设备的六种不同状态。因此，Fireface UCX II 能够完全独立运行，无需连接任何计算机。在独立操作中，只需单击一个按钮，它就可以转换为完全不同的设备。此外，TotalMix FX 以及下面显示的应用示例也可以通过 MIDI 进行控制，请参阅第 28.6 章“独立 MIDI 控制”。

23.2 Settings at the Unit
The Fireface UCX II has a simple menu structure, depending on the button pressed.

MIC/GAIN. First push brings up the Mic.Gain 1/2, second push Line/Instr. Gain 3/4, third push returns to the meter screen.

REC/PLAY. Brings up the Record/Playback (Direct USB Recording) menu, second Rec/Play Set- tings, third push returns to the meter screen.

CHAN/MIX. First push brings up the Channel menu, second push the Mix to menu, third push returns to the meter screen.

The Mix to menu gives access to the whole submix functionality, mix destination and source. A source can be modified in mix level and panorama.

SETUP/REV. First push brings up the Options menu with access to the subpages Clock, Load/Store all Settings, Hardware/Diagnosis and Control Room. Second push brings up Re- verb/Echo with the subpages Reverb and Echo.

23.2 设备上的设置
Fireface UCX II 具有简单的菜单结构，具体取决于所按下的按钮。

MIC/GAIN。第一次按下可显示 Mic.Gain 1/2，第二次按下可显示 Line/Instr. Gain 3/4，第三次按下可返回到仪表屏幕。

REC/PLAY。显示 Record/Playback（直接 USB 录音）菜单，第二次按下 Rec/Play Settings，第三次按下可返回到仪表屏幕。

CHAN/MIX。第一次按下可显示 Channel 菜单，第二次按下可显示 Mix to 菜单，第三次按下可返回到仪表屏幕。

Mix to 菜单可访问整个子混音功能、混音目标和源。可以在混音级别和全景中修改源。

SETUP/REV。第一次按下可显示 Options 菜单，可访问子页面 Clock、Load/Store all Settings、Hardware/Diagnosis 和 Control Room。第二次按下可显示 Reverb/Echo，其中包含子页面 Reverb 和 Echo。

23.3 Store / Load Setups at the Unit

tings. First select the preferred memory slot, then the desired process (load or store). The

23.3 在设备上存储/加载设置
按下 SETUP/REV 键后，编码器将允许访问加载/存储所有设置子页面。首先选择首选内存插槽，然后选择所需的过程（加载或存储）。只需按下编码器一秒钟以上即可完成加载/存储过程。

UCX II 有六个内存插槽可供免费使用。三个固定插槽可方便快速地重置和配置设备：

出厂。将设备设置为出厂默认状态。

清除混音。执行 TotalMix FX 的完全重置。

23.4 Storing Setups from the Computer

25. Routing and Monitoring
25.1 Overview
The Fireface UCX II includes a powerful digital real-time mixer, the Fireface UCX II mixer, based on RME’s unique, sample-rate independent TotalMix technology. It allows for practically unlim- ited mixing and routing operations, with all inputs and playback channels simultaneously, to any hardware outputs. TotalMix FX adds 3-band parametric Equalizer, Low Cut, Echo, Reverb, Com- pressor, Expander and Auto Level.				
				
25. 路由和监控
25.1 概述
Fireface UCX II 包含一个功能强大的数字实时混音器，即 Fireface UCX II 混音器，它基于 RME 独特的、独立于采样率的 TotalMix 技术。它允许几乎无限地进行混音和路由操作，所有输入和播放通道同时连接到任何硬件输出。TotalMix FX 添加了 3 频段参数均衡器、低切、回声、混响、压缩器、扩展器和自动电平。

Here are some typical applications for TotalMix:
● Setting up delay-free submixes (headphone mixes). The Fireface UCX II allows for up to 10 fully independent stereo submixes. On an analog mixing desk, this would equal 20 Aux sends.

● Unlimited routing of inputs and outputs (free utilisation, patchbay functionality).

● Distributing signals to several outputs simultaneously. TotalMix offers state-of-the-art splitter and distributor functions.

● Simultaneous playback of different programs via a single stereo output. The ASIO multi-client driver supports the usage of several programs at the same time. If this happens on different playback channels TotalMix provides the means to mix and monitor these on a single stereo output.

● Mixing of the input signal to the playback signal (complete ASIO Direct Monitoring). RME is not only the pioneer of ADM, but also offers the most complete implementation of the ADM functions.

● Integration of external devices. Use TotalMix to insert external effects devices, be it in the playback or in the record path. Depending on the current application, the functionality equals insert or effects send and effects return, for example as used during real-time monitoring when adding some reverb to the vocals.

以下是 TotalMix 的一些典型应用：
● 设置无延迟子混音（耳机混音）。Fireface UCX II 允许最多 10 个完全独立的立体声子混音。在模拟混音台上，这相当于 20 个辅助发送。

● 无限的输入和输出路由（免费使用，配线架功能）。

● 同时将信号分配到多个输出。TotalMix 提供最先进的分离器和分配器功能。

● 通过单个立体声输出同时播放不同的程序。ASIO 多客户端驱动程序支持同时使用多个程序。如果这种情况发生在不同的播放通道上，TotalMix 提供在单个立体声输出上混合和监控这些通道的方法。

● 将输入信号混合到播放信号（完整的 ASIO 直接监控）。RME 不仅是 ADM 的先驱，而且还提供最完整的 ADM 功能实现。

● 外部设备集成。使用 TotalMix 插入外部效果设备，无论是在播放中还是在录音路径中。根据当前应用程序，功能相当于插入或效果发送和效果返回，例如在实时监控期间为人声添加一些混响时使用。

Every single input channel, playback channel and hardware output features a Peak and RMS level meter, calculated in hardware. These level displays are very useful to determine the pres- ence and routing destinations of the audio signals.

每个输入通道、播放通道和硬件输出都配有峰值和 RMS 电平表，由硬件计算得出。这些电平显示对于确定音频信号的存在和路由目的地非常有用。

For a better understanding of the TotalMix mixer you should know the following: 

● As shown in the block diagram (next page), the record signal usually stays un-altered. TotalMix does not reside within the record path, and does not change the record level or the audio data to be recorded (exceptions: EQ+D for Record and Loopback mode).

● The hardware input signal can be passed on as often as desired, even with different levels.

This is a big difference to conventional mixing desks, where the channel fader always controls the level for all routing destinations simultaneously.

● The level meters of input and playback channels are connected pre-fader, to be able to visually monitor where a signal is currently present. The level meters of the hardware outputs are connected post-fader, thus displaying the actual output level.

为了更好地理解 TotalMix 混音器，您应该了解以下内容：

● 如框图（下一页）所示，录音信号通常保持不变。TotalMix 不驻留在录音路径中，也不会更改录音电平或要录制的音频数据（例外：录音和环回模式的 EQ+D）。

● 硬件输入信号可以按需要多次传递，即使电平不同也可以。

这与传统混音台有很大不同，传统混音台的通道推子始终同时控制所有路由目的地的电平。

● 输入和播放通道的电平表连接在推子前，以便能够直观地监控信号当前存在的位置。硬件输出的电平表连接在推子后，从而显示实际输出电平。

In some cases it is necessary to synchronize the gain changes of these routings. 在某些情况下，需要同步这些路由的增益变化。一个例子是后推子功能，其中歌手音量的变化应与发送到混响设备的信号的音量变化相同，以便混响水平保持与原始信号的关系。另一个例子是路由到不同子混音的吉他信号，这意味着硬件输出在独奏部分时声音太大，因此需要同时降低所有输出的音量。单击 Trim 按钮后，可以轻松完成此操作，并获得完美的概览。

As all channel’s routings change simultaneously when Trim is active, this mode basically causes the same behaviour as a trim pot within the input channel, affecting the signal already before the mixer. That’s how this function got its name.

由于在启用 Trim 时所有通道的路由都会同时更改，因此此模式基本上会导致与输入通道内的微调电位器相同的行为，影响混频器之前的信号。这就是此功能名称的由来。

In the View Options / Show the function Trim Gains can be globally switched on and off for all channels. The global Trim mode is recommended when using TotalMix FX as live mixing desk.

在“查看选项/显示”中，可以全局打开和关闭所有通道的“修剪增益”功能。使用 TotalMix FX 作为现场混音台时，建议使用全局修剪模式。

The Context Menu. With a right click on the input, playback and output channels their context menus provide advanced functionality (these menus are also available in the Matrix, but only directly on the channel labels). The entries are self-explanatory and automatically adjust to where the click is performed. On all of these the first entry opens the Channel Layout dialog. The input channels offer Clear, Copy input, paste the input mix and paste its FX. On a playback channel Copy, Paste and Clear the playback mix are available. On an output channel Copy and Mirror functionality for the current submix and copying of the FX settings is offered.

上下文菜单。右键单击输入、播放和输出通道，其上下文菜单可提供高级功能（这些菜单也可在 Matrix 中使用，但只能直接在通道标签上使用）。条目一目了然，并会自动调整到单击的位置。在所有这些条目中，第一个条目打开“通道布局”对话框。输入通道提供清除、复制输入、粘贴输入混音和粘贴其 FX。在播放通道上，可以使用复制、粘贴和清除播放混音。在输出通道上，提供当前子混音的复制和镜像功能以及 FX 设置的复制。
