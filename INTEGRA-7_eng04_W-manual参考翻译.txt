01 Main Features
The INTEGRA-7 is a completely new sound module that contains expressive and high-quality SuperNATURAL sounds in addition to a rich sound library from the ever-popular SRX series, as well as “Motional Surround,” which uses RSS (Roland Sound Space) technology to offer a new type of sound field.

The powerful SuperNATURAL sounds are reproduced using Motional Surround in a deeper and even more natural sound field, allowing a truly new dimension of sound creation.

快速指南
——————————————————————————————————————————
01 主要特点
INTEGRA-7 是一个全新的音效模块，除了广受欢迎的 SRX 系列丰富的音效库外，还包含富有表现力的高品质 SuperNATURAL 音效，以及使用 RSS（Roland Sound Space）技术提供新型声场的“Motional Surround”。

强大的 SuperNATURAL 音效通过 Motional Surround 在更深、更自然的声场中再现，从而实现真正全新的声音创作维度。

SuperNATURAL tones provide not only realistic sound, but also rich, natural expression during a performance
SuperNATURAL 音色不仅提供逼真的声音，而且在表演过程中提供丰富、自然的表达

####
SuperNATURAL Acoustic Tones
The SuperNATURAL acoustic tones built into the INTEGRA-7 do not merely reproduce the sound of acoustic instruments, they also use Behavior Modeling Technology to simulate the characteristic behavior of an instrument when it is played.

A dedicated sound engine tuned specifically for each type of instrument analyzes the phrases performed by the player, automatically differentiating between chordal and melodic playing to produce the optimal expression for a performance.

For example, the “violin” SuperNATURAL acoustic tone allows your conventional keyboard playing to create a realistically expressive solo violin sound that sounds as if it were being played by a violinist.

####
超自然原声音色
INTEGRA-7 内置的超自然原声音色不仅能重现原声乐器的声音，还使用行为建模技术模拟乐器演奏时的特征行为。

专门为每种乐器调校的专用声音引擎会分析演奏者演奏的乐句，自动区分和弦和旋律演奏，以产生最佳的演奏表达。

例如，“小提琴”超自然原声音色可让您通过传统的键盘演奏创造出逼真的独奏小提琴声音，听起来就像小提琴手演奏的一样。

####
SuperNATURAL Synth Tones
These are SuperNATURAL synth tones, which realistically reproduce sounds ranging from vintage analog synths to digital synths. Each synth tone consists of three sets of OSC, FILTER, AMP, and LFO, meaning that powerful synth sounds can be created using just a single synth tone.

In addition, the behaviors of the oscillators and filters of analog synths have been analyzed, allowing their distinctive sounds to be reproduced. The INTEGRA-7 contains more than 1,000 sounds, which cover every type of synth sound from analog to digital.

####
超自然合成音
这些是超自然合成音，可真实再现从老式模拟合成器到数字合成器的各种声音。每个合成音由三组 OSC、FILTER、AMP 和 LFO 组成，这意味着仅使用一个合成音即可创建强大的合成音。

此外，还分析了模拟合成器的振荡器和滤波器的行为，从而可以再现其独特的声音。INTEGRA-7 包含 1,000 多种声音，涵盖从模拟到数字的所有类型的合成音。

####
SuperNATURAL Drum Kits
The SuperNATURAL drum kits built into the INTEGRA-7 provide not only realistic, high- quality sound, they also boast smooth tonal change in response to your playing dynamics or to repeated strikes.
For example, the crash cymbal can be played not only by a powerful strike, but will also respond to a light touch. The ride cymbal will shift through a variety of tones in response to your varying dynamics. The drum will also respond appropriately to repeated strikes, so press rolls, flams, and fill-ins will sound natural.

####
SuperNATURAL 鼓组
INTEGRA-7 内置的 SuperNATURAL 鼓组不仅提供逼真的高品质声音，还可以根据演奏力度或反复敲击做出平滑的音调变化。
例如，碎音镲不仅可以通过有力的敲击来演奏，而且也可以对轻触做出反应。叠奏镲会根据您不同的力度变化而改变各种音调。鼓组也会对反复敲击做出适当反应，因此压音、装饰音和填充音听起来会很自然。

####
What is SuperNATURAL?
These are proprietary Roland sounds created using Behavior Modeling Technology, which enables natural and rich expression that was difficult to achieve on earlier sound generators.

####
什么是 SuperNATURAL？
这些是使用行为建模技术创建的专有 Roland 声音，可以实现早期声音发生器难以实现的自然而丰富的表达。

####
What is Behavior Modeling Technology?
Not only physical modeling of the instruments, Roland takes it a step further by modeling the instrument’s distinctive behavior that responds to how the performer plays, resulting in true-to-life, expressive sounds in realtime.

####
什么是行为建模技术？
Roland 不仅对乐器进行物理建模，还更进一步，对乐器的独特行为进行建模，以响应演奏者的演奏方式，从而实时产生逼真、富有表现力的声音。	

The INTEGRA-7 brings together the classic sounds of Roland synthesizers (PCM Tones)
INTEGRA-7 汇集了 Roland 合成器的经典音色（PCM 音调）


Classic preset sounds from past models of Roland synthesizers, as well as the sounds from the entire SRX series wave expansion boards have been included, especially tuned for the INTEGRA-7.

The sound data includes PCM synth tones and PCM drum kits, and can be called up from the preset banks and expansion banks.

The sounds of the SRX series are organized into SRX-01 through SRX-12 just as on the actual SRX models; when you load an entire title of sound data into one of the four virtual slots, those sounds will be available for use.

包括 Roland 合成器过去型号的经典预设音色以及整个 SRX 系列波形扩展板的音色，特别针对 INTEGRA-7 进行了调整。

音色数据包括 PCM 合成音色和 PCM 鼓组，可从预设库和扩展库中调用。

SRX 系列的音色与实际 SRX 型号一样，被组织到 SRX-01 到 SRX-12 中；当您将整个音色数据标题加载到四个虚拟插槽之一时，这些音色将可供使用。

####
PCM Synth Tones
These are sounds that were called “patches” on previous Roland synthesizers; they have been tuned specifically for the INTEGRA-7. One PCM synth tone can consist of up to four partials (waves).

####
PCM 合成音
这些声音在之前的 Roland 合成器上被称为“音色”；它们专门针对 INTEGRA-7 进行了调整。一个 PCM 合成音最多可包含四个分音（波）。

####
PCM Drum Kits
These were called “rhythm sets” on previous Roland synthesizers; they have been tuned specifically for the INTEGRA-7. A drum kit is a group of multiple percussion instrument sounds assigned so that a different percussion instrument will be heard depending on the key (note number) you play.

####
PCM 鼓组
这些在之前的 Roland 合成器上被称为“节奏组”；它们已专门针对 INTEGRA-7 进行了调整。鼓组是一组分配的多种打击乐器声音，因此根据您演奏的键（音符编号）会听到不同的打击乐器。

####
Multi-effects Usable for 16 Parts Simultaneously
All of the tones (SuperNATURAL tones and PCM tones) built into the INTEGRA-7 includes dedicated multi-effect settings.

Since a multi-effect can be used by each of the 16 parts simultaneously, the most appropriate multi-effect settings will be called up when you select a tone.

####
可同时为 16 个声部使用多重效果
INTEGRA-7 内置的所有音调（SuperNATURAL 音调和 PCM 音调）均包含专用的多重效果设置。

由于 16 个声部中的每一个都可以同时使用多重效果，因此当您选择音调时，将调用最合适的多重效果设置。

####
Motional Surround lets you control not only left/right panning but also depth

The INTEGRA-7 supports two-channel output as well as 5.1 channel surround.

Using the Motional Surround function gives you even more freedom to manipulate the spatial position of each part (p. 16).

Until now, sounds in a MIDI sound module could only be panned between left and right. However, the INTEGRA-7 lets you specify the depth as well, allowing you to create a sound field that has unprecedented depth and spaciousness.

For example, a solo instrument can be placed nearby, while the ensemble or percussion instruments are placed at a distance, thus creating a sound field with greater depth; or you can create a perceptual space that extends beyond the actual speakers.

This effect can be applied to external input signals as well, so you can use it on audio tracks as well as on the sounds of the sound module.
* To use the Motional Surround function, turn the [MOTIONAL SURROUND] button ON (p. 16, p. 34).

####
动态环绕不仅让您控制左右声像，还能控制深度

INTEGRA-7 支持双声道输出以及 5.1 声道环绕。

使用动态环绕功能，您可以更自由地操纵每个部分的空间位置（第 16 页）。

到目前为止，MIDI 音效模块中的声音只能在左右之间进行声像。但是，INTEGRA-7 还允许您指定深度，让您能够创建具有前所未有的深度和空间感的声场。

* 要使用动态环绕功能，请打开 [动态环绕] 按钮（第 16 页、第 34 页）。

####
What is Motional Surround?
In the sounds we hear every day, the “direct sound,” which reaches our ears directly from the sound source, is combined with the “reflected sound,” which has bounced off various objects in the environment. The combination of these two types of sound gives us a sense of the distance between us and the sound source. Motional Surround takes into account the state of the actual room, and simulates the way in which objects in the room reflect the sound, thus creating a three-dimensional sound field.

In order to create the most realistic sound field, the INTEGRA-7 contains a processor that generates a sense of distance, reverberation, and directionality, and also performs format conversion.

As appropriate for your playback system, Motional Surround supports both 5.1 channel and two-channel output.

####
什么是 Motional Surround？
在我们每天听到的声音中，从声源直接到达我们耳朵的“直达声”与从环境中各种物体反弹的“反射声”相结合。这两种声音的结合让我们感受到我们与声源之间的距离。Motional Surround 考虑了实际房间的状态，并模拟了房间中物体反射声音的方式，从而创建了三维声场。

为了创建最逼真的声场，INTEGRA-7 包含一个处理器，可产生距离感、混响和方向感，并执行格式转换。

根据您的播放系统，Motional Surround 支持 5.1 声道和双声道输出。

About the monitoring environment
• An acoustically dead space with minimal reverberation is ideal as a monitoring room.
• You should monitor at the sweet spot of your speaker system.

Concerning perception of a sound image’s location
• The ease with which one can sense where a sound emanates from can differ depending on the sound.
For example, it is difficult to perceive the spatial location of low-frequency sounds or sounds that have relatively little harmonic content (such as sine waves). Conversely, it tends to be easier to determine the location of sounds having rich harmonic content and a distinct attack.
• Perception of front/back localization can differ between individuals.

关于对声像位置的感知
• 人们感知声音来源的难易程度可能因声音而异。
例如，很难感知低频声音或谐波含量相对较少的声音（如正弦波）的空间位置。相反，往往更容易确定谐波含量丰富且攻击性明显的声音的位置。

####
What is Motional Surround?
The INTEGRA-7 supports two-channel and 5.1 channel surround output. By using the Motional Surround function, you can freely and easily control the placement of each part (p. 34).

While conventional systems allow sound to be placed only in the left/right direction, this new function adds the front/back dimension, enabling you to play sounds that you’ve freely placed in a soundscape with depth. Additionally, you can specify the type and size of room; this allows you to adjust the acoustics as appropriate for your song, ranging from an intimate room to a large concert hall.
####
什么是动态环绕？
INTEGRA-7 支持双声道和 5.1 声道环绕输出。通过使用动态环绕功能，您可以自由轻松地控制每个部分的放置（第 34 页）。

虽然传统系统只允许声音放置在左/右方向，但此新功能增加了前后维度，使您能够播放自由放置在具有深度的音景中的声音。此外，您可以指定房间的类型和大小；这允许您根据您的歌曲调整音响效果，范围从私密的房间到大型音乐厅。

Getting Acquainted with the INTEGRA-7
####
Studio Sets
Studio Sets allow you to use multiple tones and drum kits simultaneously.

One studio set contains 16 “parts” for tones and one external part (EXT PART). You can assign a tone or drum kit to each part, allowing you to create an ensemble or to play rich-sounding layers.

If you’re using an external MIDI device or DAW software, you’ll be able to independently control multiple sounds on a single INTEGRA-7 unit.

熟悉 INTEGRA-7
####
录音室套装
录音室套装允许您同时使用多种音色和鼓组。

一个录音室套装包含 16 个音色“部分”和一个外部部分 (EXT PART)。您可以为每个部分分配一个音色或鼓组，这样您就可以创建合奏或演奏音色丰富的层次。

如果您使用外部 MIDI 设备或 DAW 软件，您将能够独立控制单个 INTEGRA-7 设备上的多种声音。

####
How a Tone Is Structured
A “tone” is a unit of sound used when performing. There are “SuperNATURAL tones” and “PCM tones,” each containing their own effect (MFX) settings.

You need to select a tone from the sound library and assign it to a part. You can also edit a tone and save it in user memory.

####
音调的结构
“音调”是演奏时使用的声音单位。有“SuperNATURAL 音调”和“PCM 音调”，每种音调都包含自己的效果 (MFX) 设置。

您需要从音效库中选择音调并将其分配给某个部分。您还可以编辑音调并将其保存在用户内存中。

####
SuperNATURAL Tone
There are three types of SuperNATURAL tones: “SuperNATURAL acoustic tones,” “SuperNATURAL synth tones,” and “SuperNATURAL drum kits.”

####
SuperNATURAL 音色
SuperNATURAL 音色有三种类型：“SuperNATURAL 原声音色”、“SuperNATURAL 合成音色”和“SuperNATURAL 鼓组”。

####
SuperNATURAL Acoustic Tone (p. 38) These tones do not merely reproduce the sound of acoustic instruments, they also use behavior modeling technology appropriate for the selected tone to emulate the expressive power distinctive of each acoustic instrument, such as the phrases performed by the player and the differences between chords and melodic phrases.

####
SuperNATURAL 原声音色 (第 38 页) 这些音色不仅重现原声乐器的声音，还使用适合所选音色的行为建模技术来模拟每种原声乐器独特的表现力，例如演奏者演奏的乐句以及和弦与旋律乐句之间的差异。

####
SuperNATURAL Synth Tone (p. 38)
These tones consist of three partials, and allow powerful synth sounds to be produced by a single tone.

####
SuperNATURAL 合成音 (第 38 页)
这些音调由三个泛音组成，允许通过单个音调产生强大的合成音。

####
SuperNATURAL Drum Kit (p. 39)
These tones consist of 62 partials; they allow you to sound a different percussion instrument for each key (note number) that’s played.

A drum kit is provided with six compressor + equalizer units, allowing you to divide the instruments into groups so that you can individually adjust the dynamics of each group or modify its tone quality.

####
SuperNATURAL 鼓组（第 39 页）
这些音调由 62 个泛音组成；它们允许您为弹奏的每个键（音符编号）发出不同的打击乐器的声音。

鼓组配有六个压缩器 + 均衡器单元，允许您将乐器分成几组，以便您可以单独调整每组的动态或修改其音质。

####
PCM Tone
There are two types of PCM tones: “PCM synth tones” and “PCM drum kits.”
PCM Synth Tone (p. 39)
These tones consist of four partials. Each partial can be turned on/off individually, allowing you to choose which partials will be heard.
PCM Drum Kit (p. 40)
These consist of 88 partials, and allow for the play of a different percussion instrument sound for each key (note number) you press. A drum kit is provided with six compressor + equalizer units, allowing you to divide the instruments into groups so that you can individually adjust the dynamics of each group or modify its tone quality.
* The six compressor + equalizer units for a drum kit are applied only to one specified part of the multiple parts to which a drum kit can be assigned.
Make this setting in STUDIO SET COMMON (p. 32).
* Dedicated multi-effect settings are included in each tone (SuperNATURAL tones and PCM tones) built into the INTEGRA-7.

####
PCM 音调
PCM 音调有两种类型：“PCM 合成音调”和“PCM 鼓组”。
PCM 合成音调（第 39 页）
这些音调由四个分音组成。每个分音都可以单独打开/关闭，让您选择要听到的分音。
PCM 鼓组（第 40 页）
它们由 88 个分音组成，并允许您按下的每个键（音符编号）播放不同的打击乐器声音。鼓组配有六个压缩器 + 均衡器单元，允许您将乐器分成几组，以便您可以单独调整每组的动态或修改其音质。
* 鼓组的六个压缩器 + 均衡器单元仅应用于可以分配鼓组的多个部分中的一个指定部分。
在 STUDIO SET COMMON（第 32 页）中进行此设置。
* INTEGRA-7 内置的每种音调（SuperNATURAL 音调和 PCM 音调）都包含专用的多重效果设置。

####
EXPANSION Virtual Slots
EXPANSION Virtual Slots are banks into which you can load “EXPANSION SOUNDS” sound data for use.

“SRX tones,” “SuperNATURAL tones,” or “ExPCM (GM2 compatible) tones” can be loaded into an EXPANSION Virtual Slot. “ExPCM tones” use all four slots, meaning that they do not allow other tones to be loaded.

For details, refer to “04 Using the Expansion Sounds” (p. 14).

####
扩展虚拟插槽
扩展虚拟插槽是可以加载“扩展音色”声音数据以供使用的音库。

“SRX 音色”、“SuperNATURAL 音色”或“ExPCM（GM2 兼容）音色”可以加载到扩展虚拟插槽中。“ExPCM 音色”使用所有四个插槽，这意味着它们不允许加载其他音色。

有关详细信息，请参阅“04 使用扩展音色”（第 14 页）。

How a Studio Set Is Structured
A studio set has sixteen parts; you can assign a tone to each part, allowing you to use sixteen sounds simultaneously.

* Although you are free to assign a drum kit to any or all of the parts, we have assigned a drum kit to part 10 and a tone to the remaining parts as an example.

####
录音室套装的结构
录音室套装有 16 个部分；您可以为每个部分分配一个音调，这样您就可以同时使用 16 种声音。

* 虽然您可以自由地为任何或所有部分分配一套鼓，但我们已为第 10 部分分配一套鼓，并为其余部分分配一个音调，以此为例。

Bank
Tones are organized into groups called “banks.”

Part
A “part” is a location to which you can assign a tone or drum kit in order to play it on the INTEGRA-7.

Partial
A “partial” is a unit of sound within a SuperNATURAL synth tone (p. 38), SuperNATURAL drum kit (p. 39), PCM synth tone (p. 39), or PCM drum kit (p. 40). These tones consist of multiple partials.

Inst
An “inst” is the smallest unit of sound within a SuperNATURAL acoustic tone (p. 38). Each SuperNATURAL acoustic tone consists of a single “inst.”

音库
音色被组织成称为“音库”的组。

声部
“声部”是您可以将音色或鼓组分配到的位置，以便在 INTEGRA-7 上播放。

部分

音符
“音符”是 SuperNATURAL 原声音色（第 38 页）中最小的声音单位。每个 SuperNATURAL 原声音色都由一个“音符”组成。

Multi-effects (MFX)

About Memory
Memory is where data such as the settings for studio sets and tones are held.

There are three types of memory: “temporary memory” (the temporary area, and the EXPANSION Virtual Slots), “rewritable memory” (user memory, system memory, and USB memory), and “non-rewritable memory” (preset memory).

####
关于内存
内存是保存诸如工作室设置和音调设置等数据的地方。

内存有三种类型：“临时内存”（临时区域和 EXPANSION 虚拟插槽）、“可重写内存”（用户内存、系统内存和 USB 内存）和“不可重写内存”（预设内存）。

####
Temporary memory
#####
Temporary Area
This area temporarily holds the data of the studio set or tone that you’ve selected from the panel.
####
关于内存
内存是保存诸如录音室设置和音调设置等数据的地方。

内存有三种类型：“临时内存”（临时区域和 EXPANSION 虚拟插槽）、“可重写内存”（用户内存、系统内存和 USB 内存）和“不可重写内存”（预设内存）。####
临时内存
#####
临时区域
此区域临时保存您从面板中选择的录音室设置或音调的数据。

When you play, the INTEGRA-7 produces sound according to the settings in the temporary area. When you edit a studio set or a tone, you’re not directly modifying the data in that memory; rather, the data is called into the temporary area, and your editing modifies this data.

Settings in the temporary area are temporary, and will be lost when you turn off the unit or call up other settings. If you want to keep the settings of the temporary area, you must execute the Write operation (p. 29) to save them into rewritable User Memory.

当您演奏时，INTEGRA-7 会根据临时区域中的设置发出声音。当您编辑录音室设置或音调时，您不会直接修改该内存中的数据；相反，数据会被调入临时区域，您的编辑会修改这些数据。

临时区域中的设置是临时的，当您关闭设备或调用其他设置时，这些设置将会丢失。如果您想保留临时区域的设置，您必须执行写入操作（第 29 页）以将其保存到可重写的用户内存中。

#####
EXPANSION Virtual Slots
Sound data from EXPANSION SOUNDS can be loaded into these memory locations to expand the available sounds.

You can load four titles of SRX or SuperNATURAL sound data into these virtual slots.

#####
扩展虚拟插槽
可以将来自扩展声音的声音数据加载到这些内存位置以扩展可用的声音。

您可以将四个 SRX 或 SuperNATURAL 声音数据加载到这些虚拟插槽中。

Rewritable memory
#####
System Memory
This is memory that holds the system parameter settings that specify how the INTEGRA-7 is to operate. To store the system parameters, execute the System Write operation (p. 48).

####
可重写内存
#####
系统内存
此内存用于保存指定 INTEGRA-7 如何运行的系统参数设置。要存储系统参数，请执行系统写入操作（第 48 页）。

####
User Memory
This is memory that stores data inside the INTEGRA-7. To store a studio set, execute Studio Set Write (p. 34). To store a tone, execute Tone Write (p. 40). To store a drum kit, execute Drum Kit Write (p. 40).

####
用户内存
这是存储 INTEGRA-7 内部数据的内存。要存储录音室套装，请执行录音室套装写入 (p. 34)。要存储音色，请执行音色写入 (p. 40)。要存储鼓组，请执行鼓组写入 (p. 40)。

* EXPANSION SOUNDS sound data that you’ve edited in the temporary area can be saved in user memory, but it will not be audible unless the EXPANSION SOUNDS used by that sound data have been loaded into an EXPANSION Virtual Slot.

* 您在临时区域中编辑的扩展声音数据可以保存在用户内存中，但除非该声音数据所使用的扩展声音已加载到扩展虚拟插槽中，否则将无法听到。

USB Memory
You can use a USB flash drive to back up all of the studio sets, tones, and drum kits in User Memory, together with the system parameters from System Memory, as a single collection of data (p. 52).

Of the studio sets and tones saved in the INTEGRA-7, you can select the desired sound data and export it to a USB flash drive. You can also select a desired studio set or tone from a file that was previously exported to a USB flash drive, and import it.

#####
USB 存储器
您可以使用 USB 闪存驱动器将用户存储器中的所有录音室套装、音色和鼓组以及系统存储器中的系统参数备份为单个数据集合（第 52 页）。

在 INTEGRA-7 中保存的录音室套装和音色中，您可以选择所需的声音数据并将其导出到 USB 闪存驱动器。您还可以从之前导出到 USB 闪存驱动器的文件中选择一个所需的录音室套装或音色，然后将其导入。

####
Non-rewritable Memory
Preset Memory
The data in preset memory cannot be rewritten.
However, settings loaded from preset memory into the temporary area can be edited and then stored in user memory.

####
不可重写内存
预设内存
预设内存中的数据无法重写。
但是，从预设内存加载到临时区域的设置可以进行编辑，然后存储在用户内存中。

有关分配名称的详细信息，请参阅“分配名称”（第 29 页）。
3. 按 [ENTER] 按钮。
4. 将光标移动到保存目标，然后使用数值拨盘或 [DEC] [INC] 按钮指定保存目标。
5. 按 [ENTER] 按钮。
将出现一条消息询问“您确定吗？”
6. 要执行，请选择“OK”并按 [ENTER] 按钮。

备注
如果您决定取消而不保存，请按 [EXIT] 按钮。

注意
切勿在数据写入内存时关闭设备。
