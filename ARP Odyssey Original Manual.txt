Getting Started
入门

The Odyssey belongs to the class of Variable synthesizers. A variable synthesizer, as opposed to a Preset synthesizer, allows you to shape every aspect of a sound, from the attack and decay to the harmonic structure. Your Odyssey is equipped with controls that will let you precisely shape each and every parameter of the sound you are creating.

Odyssey 属于可变合成器类。 与预设合成器相反，可变合成器允许您塑造声音的各个方面，从起音和衰减到谐波结构。 您的 Odyssey 配备了控制器，可让您精确塑造您正在创建的声音的每个参数。

Synthesizers create sounds electronically in much the same fashion that any natural sound is created acoustically. There are definite elements of sound which, when put together in different combinations,will precisely reproduce anything from a clarinet to a jackhammer.The Odyssey is a musical instrument comprised of a number of different electronic circuits; each one is designed to control an element of sound.

合成器以电子方式创建声音，与任何自然声音通过声学创建的方式大致相同。 有一定的声音元素，当它们以不同的组合组合在一起时，将精确地再现从单簧管到手提钻的任何东西。奥德赛是一种由许多不同电子电路组成的乐器； 每一个都旨在控制声音的一个元素。 

This manual is an operational guide for the Odyssey, but it should also give you a working knowledge of electronic music functions.

本手册是 Odyssey 的操作指南，但它也应该让您了解电子音乐功能的使用知识。

WHAT KIND OF AMP WORKS BEST?
哪种放大器效果最好？

The whole idea of a synthesizer is to give you the capability to shape and control every aspect of a musical sound using the synthesizer's controls. Therefore the ideal
amplification system for synthesizers should introduce as little distortion or coloration as possible.For this reason, P.A. systems usually produce the cleanest sound with synthesizers.Likewise a bass guitar amplifier is probably the worst kind of amplification for synthesizers because bass guitar amps usually have poor high frequency response.Some lead guitar amps also have a lot of distortion and coloration.If you play your synthesizer through such an amp, your sounds will tend to be characteristic of the amplifier rather than the synthesizer.
Sometimes,however, the combination of the synthesizer and an amplifier with a great deal of its own coloration will produce just the sound you might be looking for.

合成器的整体理念是让您能够使用合成器的控制来塑造和控制音乐声音的各个方面。 因此理想
合成器的放大系统应尽可能减少失真或染色。因此，P.A. 系统通常使用合成器产生最干净的声音。同样，低音吉他放大器可能是合成器最差的一种放大，因为低音吉他放大器通常具有较差的高频响应。一些主音吉他放大器也有很多失真和染色。如果你演奏的合成器通过这样的放大器，你的声音往往是放大器而不是合成器的特征。
然而，有时，合成器和具有大量自身色彩的放大器的组合会产生您可能正在寻找的声音。

Also,don't be afraid to use accessory devices，such as phasers,fuzz-Wow pedals，equalizers and so forth with your Odyssey. You can get interesting results.The Odyssey can also be used as an accessory device for other electronic musical instruments through use of the external audio input jack.

此外，不要害怕在您的 Odyssey 中使用外设，例如移相器、Fuzz-Wow 踏板、均衡器等。 您可以获得有趣的结果。通过使用外部音频输入插孔，Odyssey 还可以用作其他电子乐器的外设。

HOw YOUR ODYSSEY WORKS
您的奥德赛如何运作

Generally speaking,all the electronic circuits in your Odyssey perform one of three basic functions:
一般来说，Odyssey 中的所有电子电路都执行以下三个基本功能之一：

1.Signal sources: the "raw" tones or noise which will ultimately be shaped into   musical sounds.
1.信号源：最终将形成音乐声音的“原始”音调或噪音。

2.Signal Modifiers: the"raw" sounds are passed through signal modifiers where the timbre (or tone quality) is changed to produce the desired sound, and
2.信号调制器：“原始”声音通过信号调制器传递，其中音色（或音质）被改变以产生所需的声音，并且

3.Controllers: devices which determine the operating characteristics of the signal sources and the signal modifiers.For instance,the keyboard is a controller which produces a voltage to tell the oscillator what pitch to create.Similarly, the ADSR envelope generator creates an attack and decay signal that controls the Voltage Controlled Filter (VCF) so that the final musical sound has an attack and decay.
3.控制器：确定信号源及信号调制器的操作特性的设备。例如，键盘是一个控制器，它产生一个电压来告诉振荡器要创建什么音高。同样，ADSR 包络发生器创建一个起音和控制压控滤波器 (VCF) 的衰减信号，使最终的音乐声音具有起音和衰减。 

All mechanical instruments work in a similar way.A violin, for instance, has a vibrating string which would be a signal source.The vibrating string corresponds to the oscillator in your Odyssey. The vibrations from the string are transmitted to the body of the violin which modifies the sound of these vibrations.The body of the violin is actually a mechanical filter and corresponds to the voltage Controlled Filter (vCF) on the Odyssey.It is the characteristic resonances of the body that give the violin its distinctive tone quality. The fingerboard，like the keyboard on your Odyssey, determines the pitch of the sound. The movement of the bow,like the ADSR Envelope Generator, determines the attack and decay characteristics of the sound.
所有机械乐器的工作方式都类似。例如，小提琴有一根振动的弦，它可以作为信号源。振动的弦对应于Odyssey中的振荡器。琴弦的振动传递到小提琴的琴身，从而改变这些振动的声音。小提琴的琴身实际上是一个机械滤波器，对应于Odyssey上的电压控制滤波器(VCF)。它是特征共振琴体赋予小提琴独特的音质。指板，就像Odyssey上的键盘一样，决定了声音的音高。弓的运动，就像ADSR包络发生器一样，决定了声音的起音和衰减​​特性。

Sound Sources
声源

The Voltage Controlled Oscillator on your Odyssey produces electrical waveforms (saw-tooth，pulse，and square waves) which are used to create a wide range of sound timbres.If a signal generated by an oscillator has the same waveform as a sound created by a traditional instrument，both will sound the same.
Odyssey上的压控振荡器产生电波形（锯齿波、脉冲波和方波），用于创建各种音色。如果振荡器产生的信号与由传统乐器发出的声音具有相同的波形 ，则两者听起来就一样。

Different waveforms have different sounds.Your Odyssey is capable of creating six basic waveforms，plus noise:
不同的波形有不同的声音。您的Odyssey能够创建六种基本波形，外加噪声：

Sawtooth wave; full, brassy
锯齿波；饱满的，类似铜管

Square wave; clarinet-like
方波：类似单簧管的 

Pulse wave; bright,nasal
脉搏波； 明亮，鼻音

Modulated pulse wave; chorus-like, rich
调制脉冲波； 合唱般的，丰富的

Sine wave; pure, whistle-like
正弦波； 纯净的，像哨子一样

various phase-synced waveforms; bright, rich
各种相位同步波形； 明亮、丰富

Modifiers are electronic devices that can process a signal and change its sound.Consequently a modifier must have an input and an output.A tone control on a hi-fi set is a simple modifier since it changes the nature of the sound signal that passes through its circuitry.
调制器是可以处理信号并改变其声音的电子设备。因此，调节器必须具有输入和输出。高保真音响设备上的音调控制是一种简单的调节器，因为它会改变通过它电路的声音信号的性质。 

The ARP ODYSSEY contains four modifiers,the Voltage Controlled Filter (VCF)，the Voltage Controlled Amplifier (VCA),the High Pass Filter (HPF)，and the Ring Modulator.Any signal that is introduced into the AUDIO MIXER,i.e.,Noise，vco or vco must pass through the VCF,HPF and the VCA before reaching the output of the ODYSSEY.
ARP ODYSSEY 包含四个调制器，压控滤波器（VCF）、压控放大器（VCA）、高通滤波器（HPF）和环形调制器。任何引入音频混音器的信号，如噪声， vco 或 vco 必须通过 VCF、HPF 和 VCA 才能到达 ODYSSEY 的输出。

Controller
控制器

Controllers are devices on the synthesizer which are used to create electrical signals which in turn control modifiers and sources on the synthesizer.For instance, the most obvious controller on the ODYSSEY is the keyboard. The keyboard produces a voltage which controls the vCo and can control the VCF.Other controllers on the ODYSSEY are the sample and hold，ADSR ENVELOPE GENERATOR，HPF,LFO,PITCH BEND CONTROL, and FOOTPEDAL. By this time you have had an opportunity to experiment with each of these controllers, so let's just review the functions of each of the controllers.
控制器是合成器上的设备，用于创建电信号，进而控制合成器上的修改器和音源。例如，ODYSSEY 上最明显的控制器是键盘。 键盘产生的电压可以控制 VCO 并可以控制 VCF。ODYSSEY 上的其他控制器是采样和保持，ADSR 包络发生器，HPF，LFO，弯音轮 和 脚踏板。 至此，您已经有机会尝试使用这些控制器中的任一个，所以让我们回顾一下每个控制器的功能。


KEYBOARD AND PEDALS:
键盘和踏板：

The KEYBOARD produces two CONTROL VOLTAGES. These voltages are the same if none or one key is depressed on the keyboard.(If no keys are depressed on the keyboard, the control voltages correspond to the last key played.) If two or more notes are depressed, the first control voltage corresponds to the lowest key depressed, and the second control voltage corresponds to the highest depressed.

KEYBOARD 产生两个控制电压。 如果在键盘上没有按下任何键或按下一个键，则这些电压是相同的。（如果键盘上没有按下任何键，则控制电压对应于最后弹奏的键。）如果按下两个或多个音符，则第一个控制电压对应于 到最低按键，第二控制电压对应最高按键。

control voltage is always connected to VCO 2. When VCO 2 has SYNC switched on,the keyboard is still connected to VCO 2, although in this case it does not directly control its frequency, but rather its harmonic content, as do the tuning sliders for VCO 2 in this case.(Try it.)
第一个控制电压始终连接到 VCO l，除非 VCO 1 设置为 L.F. 模式，在这种情况下键盘与 VCO 1 断开连接。控制电压始终连接到 VCO 2。当 VCO 2 开启 SYNC 时，键盘仍然连接到 VCO 2，尽管在这种情况下它不直接控制其频率，而是控制其谐波含量，就像调谐滑块一样 在这种情况下为 VCO 2。（试试看。）
